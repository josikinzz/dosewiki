# Nicomorphine
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Opioid agonist that is 2-3x the potency of morphine. The drug is used in a few countries medically. The drug has a rapid onset of effects due to its increased lipid solubility.

## Classification
- **Categories:** opioid, depressant, habit-forming, tentative

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 5-10mg
- **Light:** 2.5-5mg
- **Strong:** 10-20mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** minutes
- **Duration:** hours
- **After Effects:** 1-6 hours

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** All other CNS depressants.
