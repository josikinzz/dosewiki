# CRL-40-941
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A substance closely related to Adrafinil and Modafinil. It is the bis(p-fluoro) ring derivative of Adrafinil.

## Classification
- **Categories:** stimulant, tentative, nootropic, research-chemical
- **Also known as:** fladrafinil, fluoromodafinil

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 100-250mg
- **Light:** 50-100mg
- **Strong:** 250-350mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** 30-90 minutes
- **Duration:** 6-14 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-10 hours
