# AB-CHMINACA
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A synthetic cannabinoid related to AB-FUBINACA. There have been reports of death in overdose, similar to AB-FUBINACA and it has a low threshold dose. Exercise caution.

## Classification
- **Categories:** research-chemical, habit-forming, tentative

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 100-250ug
- **Heavy:** 400ug+
- **Light:** 50-100ug
- **Strong:** 250-400ug

## Duration
- **Onset:** minutes
- **Duration:** hours
- **After Effects:** hours
