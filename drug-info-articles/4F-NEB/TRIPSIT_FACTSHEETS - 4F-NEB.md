# 4F-NEB
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

The 4-Fluoro analogue of N-Ethylbuphedrone (NEB) is a part of the cathinone class.

## Dosage

### Insufflated
- **Common:** 75-150mg
- **Light:** 50-75
- **Strong:** 150-250mg.

### Oral
- **Common:** 100-150mg
- **Light:** 75-100mg
- **Strong:** 150-300mg

## Duration
- **Onset:** minutes
- **Duration:** minutes
- **After Effects:** 1-12 hours
