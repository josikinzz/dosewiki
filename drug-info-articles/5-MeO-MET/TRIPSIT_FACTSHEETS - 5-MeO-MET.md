# 5-MeO-MET
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

An obscure psychedelic drug similar in structure to 5-MeO-DMT. The drug is suspected to be first synthesised by Alexander Shulgin. The drug is likely to have similar effects to other psychedelic tryptamines.

## Classification
- **Categories:** psychedelic, research-chemical

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 15-25mg
- **Light:** 13-15mg
- **Strong:** 25mg+

### Vapourized
- **Common:** 5-8mg
- **Light:** 1-5mg
- **Strong:** 8-12mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** minutes
- **Duration:** hours
- **After Effects:** 1-2 hours

## Effects
- Euphoria
- empathy
- insight
- brightened colour
- Closed/Open eye visuals
- enhanced tactile sensation
- mental/physical stimulation
- decreased appetite
- pupil dilation
- restlessness
- change in perception
- ego softening
- sweating/chills
- muscle tension
- confusion
- insomnia
