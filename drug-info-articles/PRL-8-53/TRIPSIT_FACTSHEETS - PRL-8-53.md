# PRL-8-53
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A nootropic research chemical first synthesized in the 1970's. There is a single clinical study showing drastic improvement in mid-term memory among users, but otherwise it is severely under researched and caution should be given to any singular study. The reportedly does not have recreational value.

## Classification
- **Categories:** research-chemical, nootropic, tentative
- **Also known as:** prl853

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 5-10mg
- **Light:** 3-5mg
- **Strong:** 10-20mg+
- **Threshold:** 2-3mg

*Note:  NOTE: It is strongly recommend to start with low dosages and increase slowly.*

## Duration
- **Duration:** hours
- **After Effects:** 1-12 hours
