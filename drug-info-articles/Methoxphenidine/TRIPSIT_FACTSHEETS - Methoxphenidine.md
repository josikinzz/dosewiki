# Methoxphenidine
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A dissociative from the diarylethylamine class and a more potent analogue of diphenidine. The drug is reported to have unpredictable effects and can cause blackouts.

## Classification
- **Categories:** dissociative, research-chemical, tentative, habit-forming
- **Also known as:** 2-meo-diphenidine, methoxyphenidine, 2-mxp, mxp

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 75-120mg
- **Light:** 50-75mg
- **Strong:** 120-150mg+
- **Threshold:** 30-50mg

## Duration
- **Onset:** 30-60 minutes
- **Duration:** 6-8 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-3 hours
