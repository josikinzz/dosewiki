# 2C-IP
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Isopropyl analog (branched analog) of 2C-P with a slightly shorter duration and lower potency.

## Classification
- **Categories:** psychedelic, research-chemical, tentative

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 15-30mg
- **Heavy:** 30-45mg
- **Light:** 5-15mg
- **Threshold:** 5mg

## Duration
- **Onset:** 45-90 minutes
- **Duration:** 12-16 hours
- **After Effects:** 4-8 hours

## Effects
- Euphoria
- empathy
- insight
- brightened colour
- Closed/Open eye visuals
- enhanced tactile sensation
- mental/physical stimulation
- decreased appetite
- pupil dilation
- restlessness
- change in perception
- ego softening
- sweating/chills
- muscle tension
- confusion
- insomnia

## Additional Information
- **Test kits:** Marquis: Orange > Red. | Mecke: Yellow > Brown. | Mandelin: Green. | Ehrlich: No colour change.
