# Aminotadalafil
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

An analogue of tadafinil, better known as Cialis (Viagra). Infamous for being missold in the 'Alpha Male' sexual enhancement supplement.

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 10-20mg
- **Light:** 5-10mg
- **Strong:** 20-30mg

## Duration
- **Duration:** 4-8 hours
