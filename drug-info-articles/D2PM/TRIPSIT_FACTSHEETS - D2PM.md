# D2PM
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

An NDRI, which is reported as being rather lackluster in subjective effects.

## Classification
- **Categories:** stimulant, habit-forming, tentative
- **Also known as:** diphenylprolinol

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 20-35mg
- **Light:** 15-20mg
- **Strong:** 35-50mg+

*Note:  NOTE: These doses are tentative and it is recommended to do further research.*

## Duration
- **Onset:** 20-30 minutes
- **Duration:** 2-5 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-6 hours

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** Other stimulants.
