# Peyote
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Peyote is a small, spineless cactus that contains mescaline as its primary active chemical. It has a long history of use among the natives of Northern Mexico and Southwestern United States.

## Classification
- **Categories:** psychedelic, common

## Dosage

### Dried
- **Common:** 20-30g
- **Heavy:** 40g+
- **Light:** 10-20g
- **Strong:** 30-40g

### Fresh
- **Common:** 100-150g
- **Heavy:** 200g+
- **Light:** 50-100g

## Duration
- **Onset:** 60-120 minutes
- **Duration:** 4-8 hours
- **After Effects:** 6-8 hours
