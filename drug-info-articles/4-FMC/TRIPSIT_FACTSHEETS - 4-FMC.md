# 4-FMC
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A cathinone stimulant also known as flephedrone, appeared in 2008 marketed as a mephedrone replacement. Reportedly mild empathogenic effects.

## Classification
- **Categories:** stimulant, research-chemical, habit-forming
- **Also known as:** 4-fluoromethcathinone, fpephedrone, flephedrone, 4fmc

## Dosage

### Insufflated
- **Common:** 50-100mg
- **Light:** 25-40mg
- **Strong:** 100-150mg+

### Oral
- **Common:** 100-150mg
- **Light:** 50-100mg
- **Strong:** 150-200mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** minutes
- **Duration:** hours
- **After Effects:** 1-4 hours

## Effects
- Euphoria
- increased energy/alertness
- decreased need for sleep
- increased sociability
- mood lift
- increased libido
- excessive talking
- decreased appetite
- weight loss
- sweating
- disturbed sleep patterns
- bruxism
- visual and auditory hallucinations
- itchiness
- aggressiveness
- moodiness
