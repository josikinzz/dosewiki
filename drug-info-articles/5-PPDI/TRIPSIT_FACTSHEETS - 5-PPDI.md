# 5-PPDI
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

An obscure analogue of α-PBP, which is itself an analogue of α-PVP. This rare compound has little history of human usage. There are some trip reports that suggest it is inactive. In the case that it is active, it likely has a similar effect profile to other pyrovalerone stimulants.

## Classification
- **Categories:** stimulant, research-chemical, habit-forming, tentative

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 30-50mg
- **Heavy:** 70+mg.
- **Light:** 15-30mg
- **Strong:** 50-70mg

## Duration
- **Onset:** minutes
- **Duration:** hours
- **After Effects:** 3-6 hours

- Euphoria
- increased energy/alertness
- Decreased need for sleep
- Increased sociability
- mood lift
- increased libido
- Excessive talking
- Decreased appetite
- Weight loss
- Sweating

- Bruxia

- itchiness
