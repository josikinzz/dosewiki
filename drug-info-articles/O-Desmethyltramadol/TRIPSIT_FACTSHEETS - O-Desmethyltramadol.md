# O-Desmethyltramadol
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

An opioid analgesic which is the main active metabolite of tramadol. The drug is a µ full agonist and has additional affinity for δ (delta) and κ-opioid receptors.

## Classification
- **Categories:** opioid, research-chemical, habit-forming, tentative, depressant
- **Also known as:** odt, o-smt, o-dsmt

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 25-50mg
- **Light:** 10-25mg
- **Strong:** 50-100mg+

*Note:  NOTE: Insufflation is reported to be extremely painful.*

## Duration
- **Onset:** minutes
- **Duration:** 3-6 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-8 hours

## Effects
- Euphoria
- Dry Mouth
- Mood lift
- Itchiness
- Relaxant
- Constipation
- Pupil constriction
- Analgesia

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** All other CNS depressants.
