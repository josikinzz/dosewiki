# Nimetazepam
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A pharmaceutical benzodiazepine with hypnotic sedative effects. The drug is reported to have high amounts of use and abuse in Malaysia.

## Classification
- **Categories:** benzodiazepine, habit-forming, depressant
- **Also known as:** erimin

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 5-10mg
- **Light:** 2.5-5mg
- **Strong:** 10-20mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** 15-30 minutes
- **Duration:** 5-8 hours
- **After Effects:** 12-24 hours

## Effects
- Anxiolytic
- Sedative
- Muscle Relaxant
- Amnesia
- Dystaxia
- Hypnotic

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** All other CNS depressants.
