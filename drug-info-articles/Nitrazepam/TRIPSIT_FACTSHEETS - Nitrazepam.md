# Nitrazepam
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Hypnotic benzodiazepine that is used for short-term relief of anxiety and insomnia. The drug is reported to cause CNS depression.

## Classification
- **Categories:** benzodiazepine, habit-forming, depressant
- **Also known as:** mogadon, baronite, dormin, dreem, enzed, gentravit, hypnonex, hypnoril, hypnotex, konit, nicare, nigap, nipam, nirosun, nitavan, nithra

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 5-15mg
- **Heavy:** 20-30mg+
- **Light:** 2-5mg
- **Strong:** 15-20mg

## Duration
- **Onset:** 10-40 minutes
- **Duration:** 5-8 hours
- **After Effects:** 6-12 hours

## Effects
- Anxiolytic
- Sedative
- Muscle Relaxant
- Amnesia
- Dystaxia
- Hypnotic
