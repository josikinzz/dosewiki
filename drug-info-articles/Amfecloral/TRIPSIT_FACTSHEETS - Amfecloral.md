# Amfecloral
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Is a stimulant drug of the Phenethylamine and Amphetamine class. It was used as an appetite suppressant, yet is no longer marketed. It acts as a prodrug to both Amphetamine and Chloral Hydrate.

## Classification
- **Categories:** stimulant, depressant

## Duration
- **Onset:** 10-30 minutes
- **Duration:** 5-8 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-12 hours
