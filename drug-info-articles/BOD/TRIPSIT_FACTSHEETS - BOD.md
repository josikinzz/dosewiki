# BOD
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Beta-methoxy analogue of 2C-D, reports indicate it to be a more "mild" than the likes of 2C-x and DOx compounds. Reports on the bodyload are highly variable amongst users. Some experience an intense bodyload while others barely feel anything at all.

## Classification
- **Categories:** psychedelic, research-chemical, tentative

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 20-30mg
- **Heavy:** 30-40mg+
- **Light:** 15-20mg
- **Threshold:** 12-15mg

## Duration
- **Onset:** 60-120 minutes
- **Duration:** 8-14 hours
- **After Effects:** 3-6 hours

## Effects
- Euphoria
- empathy
- insight
- brightened colour
- Closed/Open eye visuals
- enhanced tactile sensation
- mental/physical stimulation
- decreased appetite
- pupil dilation
- restlessness
- change in perception
- ego softening
- sweating/chills
- muscle tension
- confusion
- insomnia
