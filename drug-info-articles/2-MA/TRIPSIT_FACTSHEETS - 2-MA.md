# 2-MA
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Also known as Ortetamine, this rarely seen amphetamine is approximately 1/10th as potent as dextroamphetamine. Little information exists regarding its safety or effects.

## Classification
- **Categories:** stimulant, research-chemical, habit-forming, tentative
- **Also known as:** 2-ma

## Dosage

### Insufflated
- **Common:** 30-50mg
- **Light:** 10-30mg
- **Strong:** 50-70mg+

## Duration
- **After Effects:** hours

## Effects
- Euphoria
- increased energy/alertness
- decreased need for sleep
- increased sociability
- mood lift
- increased libido
- excessive talking
- decreased appetite
- weight loss
- sweating
- disturbed sleep patterns
- bruxism
- visual and auditory hallucinations
- itchiness
- aggressiveness
- moodiness
