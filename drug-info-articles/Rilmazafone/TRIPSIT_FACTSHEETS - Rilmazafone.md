# Rilmazafone
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

The drug is prescribed in Japan for insomnia. The drug, also known as Rhythmy, is a benzodiazepine pro-drug which while inactive itself, is metabolised into the active compound in-vivo. The drug is soluble in water.

## Classification
- **Categories:** benzodiazepine, habit-forming, depressant
- **Also known as:** rhythmy

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 1-2mg
- **Light:** .5-1mg
- **Strong:** 2-4mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** 30-60 minutes
- **Duration:** 6-8 hours
- **After Effects:** 6-12 hours

## Effects
- Anxiolytic
- Sedative
- Muscle Relaxant
- Amnesia
- Dystaxia
