# 4-CBC
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

It is a potentially very neurotoxic cathinone. No scientic reports have been noted on the neurotoxicity or cardiotoxicity, please use with extreme caution.

## Classification
- **Categories:** stimulant, research-chemical, empathogen, habit-forming

## Dosage

### Insufflated
- **Common:** 50-80mg
- **Light:** 40-70mg
- **Strong:** 80-100mg.

### Oral
- **Common:** 70-100mg
- **Light:** 40-70mg
- **Strong:** 100-130mg

*Note:  Note: Be careful and read the summary before ingesting this substance.*

## Duration
- **Onset:** minutes
- **Duration:** hours
- **After Effects:** 2-6 hours

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** Drugs that can raise blood pressure, such as stimulants.
