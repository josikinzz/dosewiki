# PiPT
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A rare psychedelic tryptamine related to DPT. There is little known about the effects or safety profile of this compound.

## Classification
- **Categories:** psychedelic, research-chemical, tentative

## Dosage

### Vapourized
- **Common:** 15-20mg
- **Heavy:** 20mg+
- **Light:** 15mg
- **Threshold:** 10mg

## Duration
- **Onset:** minutes
- **Duration:** hours
- **After Effects:** 1-4 hours

## Effects
- Euphoria
- empathy
- insight
- brightened colour
- Closed/Open eye visuals
- enhanced tactile sensation
- mental/physical stimulation
- decreased appetite
- pupil dilation
- restlessness
- change in perception
- ego softening
- sweating/chills
- muscle tension
- confusion
- insomnia
