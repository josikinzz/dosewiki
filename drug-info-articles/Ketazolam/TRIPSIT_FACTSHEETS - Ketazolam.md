# Ketazolam
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

The drug is a benzodiazepine derivative, that is reported to have similar effects to diazepam, with reportedly milder side-effects. The drug is marketed in a few countries.

## Classification
- **Categories:** depressant, habit-forming

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 15-30mg
- **Light:** 5-15mg
- **Strong:** 30-60mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** 30-90 minutes
- **Duration:** 8-12 hours
- **After Effects:** 2-12 hours

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** All other CNS depressants.
