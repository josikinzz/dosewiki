# Mexedrone
*Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mexedrone*

Mexedrone (also known as 4-MMC-MeO) is a stimulant and an entactogen drug of the cathinone class that has been sold online as a designer drug. It is the alpha-methoxy derivative of Mephedrone.

## Pharmacology

Mexedrone acts as a weak serotonin–norepinephrine–dopamine reuptake inhibitor (SNDRI) with IC50 values of 5,289 nM, 8,869 nM, and 6,844 nM, respectively, as well as a weak serotonin releasing agent (SRA) with an EC50 value of 2,525 nM.

## Legal status

Mexedrone is illegal in Sweden as of January 26, 2016 as well as Japan as of August 24, 2016.
