**U.S. FEDERAL LEGAL SUMMARY**
**Clonidine**
- REGULATED: Yes
- STATUS: Prescription Only
- SCHEDULE: Un-Scheduled
 
Clonidine (Catapres) is unscheduled in the United States, but is available by prescription only. This means that sales and distribution are allowed only by those with a license and only to those with a prescription (according to FDA regulations). Possession is not illegal even without a prescription.
