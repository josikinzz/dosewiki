# PCE
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Eticyclidine (PCE) is a dissociative anesthetic that has hallucinogenic effects. It is reported to be slightly more potent than (PCP).

## Classification
- **Categories:** dissociative, tentative, habit-forming
- **Also known as:** eticyclidine

## Dosage

### Insufflated
- **Common:** 3-7mg
- **Light:** 1-3mg
- **Strong:** 7-15mg+

## Duration
- **Duration:** 2-5 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-4 hours
