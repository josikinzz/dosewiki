# Halazepam
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A less common prescription benzodiazepine and derivative of Nordazepam. Also known as Paxipam, it is no longer prescribed in the USA. The drug is reported to have sedating, hypnotic and anxiolytic effects. Doses may induce amnesia and lowered inhibitions.

## Classification
- **Categories:** benzodiazepine, habit-forming, depressant
- **Also known as:** paxipam

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 20-40mg
- **Light:** 10-20mg
- **Strong:** 40-80mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** 15-45 minutes
- **Duration:** 8-14 hours
- **After Effects:** 12-40 hours

## Effects
- Anxiolytic
- Sedative
- Muscle Relaxant
- Amnesia
- Dystaxia
- Hypnotic

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** All other CNS depressants.
