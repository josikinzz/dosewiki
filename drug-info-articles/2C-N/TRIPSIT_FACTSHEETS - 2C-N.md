# 2C-N
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A relatively uncommon and short-acting psychedelic phenethylamine of the 2C-x family. It behaves much like other 2C-x drugs, but is unique in that it is a yellowish golden-coloured salt. Described by some, namely Alexander Shulgin, as being quite underwhelming.

## Classification
- **Categories:** psychedelic, research-chemical, tentative
- **Also known as:** 2cn

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 100-125mg
- **Light:** 100mg
- **Strong:** 125-150mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** 15-45 minutes
- **Duration:** 4-6 hours
- **After Effects:** 2-4 hours

## Effects
- Euphoria
- empathy
- insight
- brightened colour
- Closed/Open eye visuals
- enhanced tactile sensation
- mental/physical stimulation
- decreased appetite
- pupil dilation
- restlessness
- change in perception
- ego softening
- sweating/chills
- muscle tension
- confusion
- insomnia
