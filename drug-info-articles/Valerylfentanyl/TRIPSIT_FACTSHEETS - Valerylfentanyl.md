# Valerylfentanyl
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Opioid that is a higher homologue of butyrfentanyl with a greatly reduced potency.

## Classification
- **Categories:** opioid, depressant, research-chemical, tentative, habit-forming
- **Also known as:** vf

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Light:** 50mg+
