# Sinicuichi
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A perennial shrub with small narrow leaves and yellow flowers growing up to ~1.5 meters. The drug is known under many different names. The drug been used for shamanic purposes by indigenous people in Central America and Mexico. The drug is reported to be mainly auditory based.

## Classification
- **Categories:** psychedelic, tentative
- **Also known as:** sini, shrubby-yellowcrest, sun-opener, heimia-salicifolia, willow-leaf-heimia

## Duration
- **Onset:** 1-5 minutes
- **Duration:** 15-45 minutes
