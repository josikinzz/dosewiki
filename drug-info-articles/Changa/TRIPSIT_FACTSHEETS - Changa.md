# Changa
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Changa is the name given to a smoking blend of Banisteriopsis caapi or other MAOI containing plants with an infusion of extracted DMT. Changa often contains other milder psychoactive herbs as well. The MAOI action results in a longer and more intense trip (see Ayahuasca) NOTE: There is no standardized recipe or concentration of infused DMT, therefore dosage and potency will vary per batch.

## Classification
- **Categories:** psychedelic, tentative

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 15-30mg
- **Heavy:** 50+mg
- **Light:** 5-15mg
- **Strong:** 30-50mg

*Note:  NOTE: These are tentative estimates as Changa potency and dosing will vary per batch/source.*

## Duration
- **Onset:** 0-2 minutes
- **Duration:** 6-12 minutes
- **After Effects:** 1-36 hours

## Effects
- immersive experience
- open eye visuals
- radical perspective shifting
- profound life-changing spiritual experiences
- powerful rushing of sensation
- change in perception of time
- auditory hallucinations
- color shifting
- hard on lungs when smoking
- stomach discomfort
- difficulty integrating experiences
- overwhelming fear

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** Combining with any medications or substance contraindicated with MAOI's particuarly SSRI's, MDxx compounds and Amphetamines. Refer to http://wiki.tripsit.me/wiki/Drug_combinations for more information.
