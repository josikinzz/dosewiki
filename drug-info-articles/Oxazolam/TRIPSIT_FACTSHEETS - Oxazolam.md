# Oxazolam
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A benzodiazepine derivative that is a prodrug for desmethyldiazepam.

## Classification
- **Categories:** depressant, habit-forming

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 10-15mg
- **Light:** 5-10mg
- **Strong:** 15-30mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** 30-90 minutes
- **Duration:** 6-12 hours
- **After Effects:** 6-12 hours
