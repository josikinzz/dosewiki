# βH-2C-B
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

β-Hydroxy-2C-B is a novel analogue of 2C-B that is a slightly less potent to 2C-B, and reportedly a longer duration. 2C-B is suspected to be the active metabolite of βk-2C-B.

## Classification
- **Categories:** psychedelic, research-chemical, tentative
- **Also known as:** beta-hydroxy-2c-b, bh-2c-b, bh-2cb, bohb, boh-2c-b, boh-2cb

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 40-60mg
- **Heavy:** 75mg+
- **Light:** 25-40mg
- **Strong:** 60-75mg

## Duration
- **Onset:** 30-50 minutes
- **Duration:** 7-10 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-12 hours
