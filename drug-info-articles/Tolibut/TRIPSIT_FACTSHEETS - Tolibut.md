# Tolibut
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Analogue of GABA and the 4-methyl analogue of phenibut. The drug is reported to have similar effects to phenibut and GABA, the drug acts on GABA(B).

## Classification
- **Categories:** depressant, habit-forming, research-chemical, tentative
