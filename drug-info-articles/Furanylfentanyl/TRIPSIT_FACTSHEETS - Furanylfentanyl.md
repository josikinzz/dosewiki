# Furanylfentanyl
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

An extremely potent opioid analgesic and analogue of fentanyl. There is extremely limited information about this drug. It is believed to be slightly less potent and shorter in duration than butyrfentanyl. Exercising extreme caution is strongly recommended.

## Classification
- **Categories:** research-chemical, habit-forming, tentative, opioid, depressant
- **Also known as:** fu-f, furanyl-fentanyl

## Dosage

### Insufflated
- **Common:** 400-800ug
- **Light:** 200-400ug
- **Strong:** 800-1600ug+

### Oral
- **Common:** 500-900ug
- **Light:** 300-500ug
- **Strong:** 900-1600ug+.

*Note:  NOTE: Very potent opioid, be very careful while dosing.*

## Duration
- **Onset:** 1-10 minutes
- **Duration:** 1-3 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-3 hours
