# 4-MTA
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Empathogen with very low chronic toxicity but high risk of acute harm due to mixed serotonin release and MAOI action. There are some reports that indicate it does not have a notable euphoric effect.

## Classification
- **Categories:** research-chemical, empathogen, tentative
- **Also known as:** 4-methylthioamphetamine
