# Ibogaine
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

An alkaloid found in many plants in Africa, most famously Iboga, that has psychedelic and hallucinogenic properties. Reported to often be unpleasant. Traditionally used in tribal environments for coming-of-age rituals, it has recently been used as an alternative treatment for substance use disorders. There is on-going research on the efficacy of this. The drug has numerous risks and has killed in overdose. Further research and caution is strongly advised.

## Classification
- **Categories:** psychedelic, opioid, dissociative, depressant
- **Also known as:** iboga

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 16-22mg/kg

## Duration
- **Onset:** 45-180 minutes
- **Duration:** 24-30 hours
- **After Effects:** 24-72 hours
