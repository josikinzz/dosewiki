# 4-AcO-DALT
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

An uncommon tryptamine derivative first reported in the mid 2000s, likely a psychedelic. Little is known about the pharmacology or effects of this compound.

## Classification
- **Categories:** psychedelic, research-chemical
- **Also known as:** 4acodalt

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 50-70mg
- **Heavy:** 100+mg
- **Light:** 40-50mg
- **Threshold:** 20mg

### Vapourized
- **Common:** 10-15mg
- **Light:** 5-8mg
- **Strong:** 15+mg
- **Threshold:** 2-4mg

## Duration
- **Onset:** 30-90 minutes
- **Duration:** 5-9 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-8 hours

## Effects
- Euphoria
- empathy
- insight
- brightened colour
- Closed/Open eye visuals
- enhanced tactile sensation
- mental/physical stimulation
- decreased appetite
- pupil dilation
- restlessness
- change in perception
- ego softening
- sweating/chills
- muscle tension
- confusion
- insomnia
