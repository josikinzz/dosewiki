# 4-MEC
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A substituted cathinone stimulant; little is known about its safety or subjective effects. It is believed to have similar effects to mephedrone, but with a lower potency and a stronger stimulating character.

## Classification
- **Categories:** stimulant, research-chemical, habit-forming
- **Also known as:** 4-Methylethcathinone, 4mec, 4-methylethcathinone

## Dosage

### Insufflated
- **Common:** 40-100mg
- **Light:** 25-40mg
- **Strong:** 100-150mg+

### Oral
- **Common:** 100-200mg
- **Light:** 50-100mg
- **Strong:** 200mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** minutes
- **Duration:** hours
- **After Effects:** 2-4 hours

## Effects
- Euphoria
- increased energy/alertness
- decreased need for sleep
- increased sociability
- mood lift
- increased libido
- excessive talking
- decreased appetite
- weight loss
- sweating
- disturbed sleep patterns
- bruxism
- visual and auditory hallucinations
- itchiness
- aggressiveness
- moodiness
