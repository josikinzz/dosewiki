# 4-FPP
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A piperazine derivative that has been reported to have mild pyschedelic effects and empathogenic qualities. It has been sold in many branded legal highs.

## Classification
- **Categories:** psychedelic, empathogen, tentative, stimulant

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 40-80mg
- **Light:** 20-40mg
- **Strong:** 80-150mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** 15-45 minutes
- **Duration:** 4-8 hours
