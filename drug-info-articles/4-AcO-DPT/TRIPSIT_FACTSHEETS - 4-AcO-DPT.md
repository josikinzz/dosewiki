# 4-AcO-DPT
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Research chemical of tryptamine class. First noted being sold in 2012. There are conflicting reports on it's effects. The drug does not dissolve well in all solutions it was tested.

## Classification
- **Categories:** psychedelic, tentative, research-chemical
- **Also known as:** 4acodpt

## Dosage

### Insufflated
- **Common:** 15-25mg
- **Light:** 10-15mg
- **Strong:** 25-35mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** minutes
- **After Effects:** 2-8 hours

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** This tryptamine may work differently than other tryptamines. Caution is recommended for all mixes with it.
