# CRL-40-940
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Bisfluoro analogue Modafinil. Sold sometimes online as a research chemical. The drug was patented in 2013. It is slightly more potent than Armodafinil.

## Classification
- **Categories:** stimulant, tentative, nootropic, research-chemical
- **Also known as:** flmodafinil, lauflumide, bisfluoromodafinil

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 40-75mg
- **Light:** 20-40mg
- **Strong:** 75-150mg.

## Duration
- **Onset:** 30-45 minutes
- **Duration:** 6-12 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-8 hours
