**U.S. FEDERAL LEGAL SUMMARY**
**Promethazine**
- REGULATED: Yes
- STATUS: Prescription Only
- SCHEDULE: Un-Scheduled
 
Promethazine is unscheduled in the United States, but is available by prescription only. This means that sales and distribution are allowed only by those with a license and only to those with a prescription (according to FDA regulations). Possession is not illegal even without a prescription.
 
U.K. (United Kingdom, Britain, England, Wales, Scotland) #
 
Promethazine is sold as a non-prescription medication to treat allergies, motion sickness, and as a sleep aid (Phenergan). (thanks jp) (last updated Apr 22 2013)
