# 5-IT
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

An empathogenic stimulant with a long duration. The drug is prone to cause overheating. Caution is recommended when using this drug. The drug is related to Î±MT, though it is described as being less psychedelic and with much stronger stimulating effects.

## Classification
- **Categories:** stimulant, psychedelic, research-chemical, habit-forming, empathogen
- **Also known as:** 5-api

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 50-100mg
- **Light:** 50mg
- **Strong:** 100-150mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** 90-120 minutes
- **Duration:** 8-12 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-48 hours

## Effects
- Euphoria
- increased energy/alertness
- Decreased need for sleep
- Increased sociability
- mood lift
- increased libido
- Excessive talking
- Decreased appetite
- Weight loss
- Sweating
- Disturbed sleep patterns
- Bruxia
- Visual and auditory hallucinations
- itchiness
- aggressiveness
- moodiness
