# 5-IAI
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A monoamine triple releaser first synthesised by David Nichols, possesses similar properties to MDMA. It is suspected to be neurotoxic however more research is needed to say.

## Classification
- **Categories:** stimulant, psychedelic, research-chemical, habit-forming, empathogen
- **Also known as:** 5iai

## Duration
- **After Effects:** 1-24 hours

## Effects
- Euphoria
- increased energy/alertness
- Decreased need for sleep
- Increased sociability
- mood lift
- increased libido
- Excessive talking
- Decreased appetite
- Weight loss
- Sweating
- Disturbed sleep patterns
- Bruxia
- Visual and auditory hallucinations
- itchiness
- aggressiveness
- moodiness
