# BZP
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A stimulant that was sold as "legal ecstasy (MDMA)" in the late 2000s, and was one of the first drugs widely marketed as a "research chemical." It has a reportedly less favourable effect profile and has fallen out of popularity greatly since it was banned in many countries.

## Classification
- **Categories:** stimulant, research-chemical, habit-forming

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 70-100mg
- **Heavy:** 130mg+
- **Light:** 40-70mg
- **Strong:** 100-130mg

## Duration
- **Onset:** 30-45 minutes
- **Duration:** 4-6 hours
- **After Effects:** 2-4 hours
