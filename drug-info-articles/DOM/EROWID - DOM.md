DOM Dosage
 
by Erowid
 
---
 
 
DOM is a phenethylamine found in tablet, liquid, or powder form. It is generally injested orally. Since DOM is very uncommon, there is limited information about dosage levels. Following are approximate dosages for pure DOM.

**Oral DOM Dosages**
- Threshold: 0.5 - 1 mg
- Light: 1 - 2 mg
- Common: 2 - 6 mg
- Strong: 6 - 10 mg
- Heavy: 8 - 12 mg
**Onset : 1 - 2 hours Duration : 12 - 20 hours (depending on dosage) Normal After Effects : 6 - 12 hours**

U.S. FEDERAL LAW #
 
Caution : All legal information should be verified through other sources. [ see below ]
 
**U.S. FEDERAL LEGAL SUMMARY**
**DOM**
- REGULATED: Yes
- STATUS: Scheduled
- SCHEDULE: Schedule I
- CLASSIFICATION: Hallucinogen
 
DOM is Schedule I in the United States. This means it is illegal to manufacture, buy, possess, or distribute (sell, trade or give) without a DEA license.
 
INTERNATIONAL LAW #
 
Belgium #
 
DOM is HOOFDSTUK II in Belgian. See [Law Belgium](https://erowid.org/psychoactives/law/countries/law_belgium.shtml) . (thanks cs) (last updated Aug 1 2011)
 
Italy (Italia) #
 
Listed in Tabella I of "Tabelle delle sostanze stupefacenti e psicotrope", making it illegal to possess, purchase, or sell. (see [Tabelle delle sostanze stupefacenti e psicotrope](http://www.salute.gov.it/portale/temi/p2_6.jsp?id=3729&area=sostanzeStupefacenti&menu=vuoto) . (thanks a) (last updated Dec 16, 2015)
 
New Zealand #
 
DOM is Schedule I (Class A) in New Zealand [ [reference](https://erowid.org/psychoactives/law/countries/nz/law_nz_info1.shtml) ].
 
Russia #
 
We have been told that DOM is Schedule I in the Russian Federation, making it illegal to buy, sell or possess without a license. (unconfirmed) (thanks d)
 
U.K. #
 
DOM is [Schedule I](https://erowid.org/psychoactives/law/countries/uk/uk_law_definitions.shtml) in the U.K., making it illegal to buy, sell or possess without a license.
