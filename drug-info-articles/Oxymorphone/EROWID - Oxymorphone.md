**U.S. FEDERAL LEGAL SUMMARY**
**Oxymorphone**
- REGULATED: Yes
- STATUS: Scheduled
- SCHEDULE: Schedule II
- CLASSIFICATION: Opioid
 
Oxymorphone is Schedule II in the U.S. This means it is illegal to sell without a DEA license and illegal to buy or possess without a license or prescription.
