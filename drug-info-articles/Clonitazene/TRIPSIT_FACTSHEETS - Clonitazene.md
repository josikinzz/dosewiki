# Clonitazene
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

An opioid that is roughly three times as potent as Morphine. Is related to Etonitazene, which is about 1000-1500x the potency of Morphine.

## Classification
- **Categories:** opioid, habit-forming, depressant, research-chemical

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 5-10mg
- **Light:** 2.5-5mg
- **Strong:** 10-15mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** minutes
- **Duration:** hours
- **After Effects:** 1-8 hours
