# Moclobemide
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A reversible MAOI which is prescribed to treat depressive and anxiety disorders. Due to its reversibility and short-acting effects it is often used in "pharmahuasca" preparations.

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 300mg
- **Light:** 150mg
- **Strong:** 600mg.

## Duration
- **Onset:** 30-90 minutes
- **Duration:** 2-6 hours
