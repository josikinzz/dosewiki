# 2-PTA
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

4-Methylamphetamine with the alpha-methyl replaced by ketone. There are very few reports and there is limited information about toxicity. As always treat with caution.

## Classification
- **Categories:** stimulant, research-chemical, habit-forming, tentative
- **Also known as:** 4-methyl-2-pa, 2-(p-tolyl)acetamide

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 20-40mg
- **Heavy:** 80+mg.
- **Light:** 10-20mg
- **Strong:** 40-80mg

## Duration
- **Onset:** 10-25 minutes
- **Duration:** 1-3 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-6 hours
