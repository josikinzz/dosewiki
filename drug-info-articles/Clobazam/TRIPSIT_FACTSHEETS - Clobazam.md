# Clobazam
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A long acting benzodiazepine which focuses primarily on anxiolytic and anticonvulsant properties. It is used medically to treat epilepsy, and this along with its relatively many negative side-effects and contraindications in comparison with other benzodiazepines means it isn't frequently used recreationally.

## Classification
- **Categories:** benzodiazepine, habit-forming, depressant
- **Also known as:** frisium

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 10-15mg
- **Heavy:** 15-30mg+
- **Light:** 5-10mg

*Note:  NOTE: 20mg of Clobazam is approximately equal to 10mg of Diazepam*

## Duration
- **Onset:** 20-40 minutes
- **Duration:** 6-12 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-12 hours

## Effects
- Anxiolytic
- Sedative
- Muscle Relaxant
- Amnesia
- Dystaxia

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** All other CNS depressants
