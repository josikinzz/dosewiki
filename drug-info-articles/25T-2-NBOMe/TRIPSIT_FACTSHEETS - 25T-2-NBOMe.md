# 25T-2-NBOMe
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A potent serotonin receptor agonist at the 5-HT2a receptor that is of the NBOMe drug class. It is a hallucinogenic and psychedelic with limited recorded human use.

## Classification
- **Categories:** psychedelic, research-chemical, tentative
- **Also known as:** 25t2-nbome

## Dosage

### Sublingual
- **Common:** 300-600ug
- **Light:** 100-300ug
- **Strong:** 600-1000ug.

## Duration
- **Onset:** 30-45 minutes
- **Duration:** 6-10 hours
- **After Effects:** 2-10 hours
