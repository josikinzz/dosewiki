# 5-MeO-Î±MT
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A long-acting psychedelic and entactogenic tryptamine. The drug is structurally related to aMT. The drug is reported to be very potent and is sometimes sold on blotters. There have been a few reports of the drug being sold as LSD, however it seems this is rare. The drug may be an MAOI, caution is advised when dosing.

## Classification
- **Categories:** stimulant, psychedelic, research-chemical, empathogen
- **Also known as:** 5meoamt

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 2-4mg
- **Light:** 1-2mg
- **Strong:** 4-6mg
- **Threshold:** 0.5-1mg

*Note:  NOTE: Hospitalizations have occured with as doses as low as 8mg*

## Duration
- **Onset:** 30-120 minutes
- **Duration:** hours
- **After Effects:** 4-24+ hours
