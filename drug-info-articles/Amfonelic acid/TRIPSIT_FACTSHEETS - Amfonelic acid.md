# Amfonelic acid
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A dopaminergic stimulant discovered by Sterling-Winthrop accidentally while developing antibiotics. Discontinued due to undesirable stimulant effects, it has had some popularity as a recreational drug.

## Classification
- **Categories:** stimulant, research-chemical, habit-forming, nootropic
- **Also known as:** aa, afa, win25978

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 4-10mg
- **Strong:** 10mg+
- **Threshold:** 2-4mg

## Duration
- **Onset:** minutes
- **Duration:** hours
- **After Effects:** 1-24 hours

## Effects

- increased energy/alertness
- Decreased need for sleep
- Increased sociability

- increased libido
- Excessive talking
- Decreased appetite
- Weight loss
- Sweating
- Disturbed sleep patterns
- Bruxia

- itchiness
