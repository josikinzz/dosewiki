# Thiopropamine
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A substance that is closely related to methiopopramine (MPA). Is roughly about 1/3rd the potency of amphetamine and differs from amphetmine from the phenyl ring in which it is switched with a thiophene ring.

## Classification
- **Categories:** stimulant, habit-forming, research-chemical
- **Also known as:** tpa, normethiopropamine, a-methyl-2-thipheneethanamine

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 50-100mg
- **Light:** 20-50mg
- **Strong:** 100-125mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** minutes
- **Duration:** hours
- **After Effects:** hours
