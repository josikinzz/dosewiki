# 5-Bromo-DMT
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Psychedelic drug of the tryptamine class, and is also found in two marine invertebrates. Animal studies have shown that this could be useful for sedative and/or antidepressant use.

## Classification
- **Categories:** psychedelic, tentative, research-chemical

## Dosage

### Vapourized
- **Common:** 25-35mg.
- **Light:** 10-25mg.
- **Strong:** 35-65mg.

## Duration
- **Onset:** 1-2 minutes
- **Duration:** 15-90 minutes
