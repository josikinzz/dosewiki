# Citalopram
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Citalopram is an SSRI that is used to treat depression. It does not lead to a high and causes a wide range of negative psychological and physical effects when people attempt to abuse it. These include nausea, serotonin syndrome, and suicidal ideation. Those with bipolar disorder tend to go into a mixed state or manic states when trying SSRI's.

## Classification
- **Categories:** common, ssri
- **Also known as:** celexa, cipramil

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 20-40mg once a day
