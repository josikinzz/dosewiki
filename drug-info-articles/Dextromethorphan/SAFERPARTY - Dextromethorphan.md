# Dextromethorphan
*Source: [SaferParty](https://saferparty.ch) - Drug Checking Service (Switzerland)*

## Effects
In high doses (120 mg or more) it has a hallucinogenic and euphoric effect. Perceptual disturbances up to confusion as well as states of agitation with epileptic episodes may occur. Side effects are reddening of the skin and severe itching. With regular use there is great potential for dependence with psychological and physical symptoms!

## Risks

### Short-term Risks
Caution: The simultaneous intake of MAO inhibitors delays the degradation of DXM and can lead to severe respiratory and circulatory disorders!
