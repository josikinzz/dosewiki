# 2-methyl-2-butanol
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A tertiary alcohol that has sedative, hypnotic, and anxiolytic effects. It is also roughly 20x more potent than Ethanol by weight.

## Classification
- **Categories:** habit-forming, depressant, tentative
- **Also known as:** 2m2b

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 5-7.5g
- **Light:** 2.5-5g
- **Strong:** 7.5-15g+

## Duration
- **Onset:** 5-25 minutes
- **Duration:** 4-8 hours
- **After Effects:** 2-6 hours

## Effects
- Anxiolytic
- Sedative
- Muscle Relaxant
- Amnesia
- Dystaxia
- Hypnotic

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** All other CNS depressants.
