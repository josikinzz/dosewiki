# Benzydamine
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

NSAID with local anesthetic and analgesic properties, similar to ibuprofen or naproxen. Sometimes abused in overdose with reported delirant and stimulant effects.

## Classification
- **Categories:** deliriant, tentative, stimulant
- **Also known as:** tantum

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 500-1000mg
- **Light:** 250-500mg
- **Strong:** 1000-1500mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** 15-60 minutes
- **Duration:** 4-8 hours
- **After Effects:** 2-48 hours
