# Dehydroxyfluorafinil
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Nootropic of the benzhydryl class. Closely related to Adrafinil and Modafinil. Branded as "Modafiendz" by some research chemical vendors.

## Classification
- **Categories:** stimulant, nootropic, research-chemical, tentative
- **Also known as:** modafiendz

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 100-150mg
- **Light:** 50-100mg
- **Strong:** 150-200mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** 30-60 minutes
- **Duration:** 5-8 hours
- **After Effects:** 2-24 hours
