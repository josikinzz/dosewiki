# Tilidine
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Synthetic opioid used mainly for the treatment of moderate to severe pain. In some countries it's mixed with naloxone to lower the abuse liability. Roughly 0.2 - 1 on the potency scale to morphine. (20mg of morphine to 100mg tilidine)

## Classification
- **Categories:** opioid, habit-forming, depressant
- **Also known as:** tilidate, tilidin, valoron, valtran

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 50-100mg
- **Light:** 25-50mg
- **Strong:** 100-200mg.

## Duration
- **Onset:** 10-20 minutes
- **Duration:** 3-6 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-12 hours

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** All other CNS depressants.
