# Scopolamine
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

The main active compound in datura. Medium and high doses may have significant negative side effects and can cause extreme deliriant hallucinations. Caution is strongly recommended.

## Classification
- **Categories:** deliriant, tentative

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** One of the active substances in datura, caution is strongly recommended with this drug.
