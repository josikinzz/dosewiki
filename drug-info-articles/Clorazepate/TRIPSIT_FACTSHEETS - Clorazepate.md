# Clorazepate
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Is a prodrug for desmethyldiazepam which is responsible for most of the therapeutic effects. Has a long half life, with the addition of desmethyldiazepam as the main metabolite, which makes it much longer.

## Classification
- **Categories:** depressant, benzodiazepine, habit-forming, depressant
- **Also known as:** tranxene, novo-clopate, tranzene

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 10-20mg
- **Light:** 5-10mg
- **Strong:** 20-40mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** 20-80 minutes
- **Duration:** 8-12 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-8 hours

## Effects
- Anxiolytic
- Sedative
- Muscle Relaxant
- Amnesia
- Dystaxia
- Hypnotic

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** All other CNS depressants.
