MDE generally comes in either tablet form or as a whitish powder. It is also frequently found in pills sold as MDMA. Following are approximate dosages in mg.
 
- Oral MDE Dosages: Oral MDE Dosages
**Oral MDE Dosages**
- Threshold: 30 - 50 mg
- Common for small or sensitive people: 80 - 120 mg
- Common for most people: 120 - 150 mg
- Common for large or unsensitive people: 150 - 200 mg
- Required by few (side effects increase): 200 + mg
 
 Duration : 2 - 4 hours 
 Normal After Effects : 3 - 6 hours
