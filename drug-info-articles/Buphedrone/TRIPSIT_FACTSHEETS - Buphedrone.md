# Buphedrone
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A cathinone stimulant first discovered in 1928, has gained some modest popularity as a mephedrone replacement. It is reported to be to be around 2-3x more potent than methcathinone.

## Classification
- **Categories:** stimulant, research-chemical, habit-forming

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 30-50mg
- **Heavy:** 50-80mg
- **Light:** 10-30mg

## Duration
- **Onset:** 10-20 minutes
- **Duration:** hours
- **After Effects:** 1-8 hours
