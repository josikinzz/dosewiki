# W-15
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A potent opioid that is 5.4x more potent than morphine. The drug has a distinct structure unrelated to nearly all other opioid compounds.

## Classification
- **Categories:** opioid, research-chemical, habit-forming, tentative, depressant

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 5-10mg
- **Light:** 2-5mg
- **Strong:** 10-15mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** 15-40 minutes
- **Duration:** 4-6 hours
- **After Effects:** 2-4 hours

## Effects
- Euphoria
- Dry Mouth
- Mood lift
- Itchiness
- Relaxant
- Constipation
- Pupil constriction
- Analgesia

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** All other CNS depressants.
