# Butyrfentanyl
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Potent short-acting opioid and fentanyl analogue. Often dispensed in a nasal spray. This is an highly potent drug - exercise extreme caution. May cause respiratory depression and death in overdose.

## Classification
- **Categories:** opioid, research-chemical, habit-forming, tentative, depressant
- **Also known as:** bf, b-f

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 800-1500ug
- **Light:** 400-800ug
- **Strong:** 1.5-3mg

*Note:  Note: The drug is reported to be potent, caution is recommended.*

## Duration
- **Onset:** minutes
- **Duration:** hours
- **After Effects:** 1-4 hours

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** All other CNS depressants.
