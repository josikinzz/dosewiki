# Indapyrophenidone
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Indane analogue of Pyrophenidone, a NDRI stimulant with a short duration.

## Classification
- **Categories:** stimulant, tentative, research-chemical, habit-forming

- **Light:** 20-40mg
- **Strong:** 60-90mg

### Vapourized

- **Heavy:** 50mg+.
