# Morpheridine
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Is a 4-Phenylpiperidine derivative that is related to pehidine. It is roughly 4x as potent as the parent compound and is reported to not cause convulsions that pethidine reportedly does.

## Classification
- **Categories:** opioid, depressant, habit-forming, tentative

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 10-20mg
- **Light:** 5-10mg.
- **Strong:** 20-35mg

## Duration
- **After Effects:** 1-6 hours
