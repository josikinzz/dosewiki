# Nefiracetam
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Nootropic compound of the racetam family. The drug is reported to enhance both GABAergic and cholinergic signaling. Long-term use is suggested to be neuroprotective. The drug is fat soluble.

## Classification
- **Categories:** nootropic, tentative, research-chemical
- **Also known as:** nerfiracetam

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 150-450mg.

*Note:  NOTE: The drug is typically divided into three doses. Roughly 0.5mg-1.6mg/kg.*

## Duration
- **Onset:** 30-60 minutes
- **Duration:** 6-12 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-8 hours
