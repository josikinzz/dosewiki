# MXM
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A arylcyclohexylamine dissociative anaesthetic closely related to methoxetamine, ketamine, and PCP. It has seen some popularity among drug users, often being advertised as an MXE replacement after its illegalisation in many parts of the world. Anectodal reports indicate a more stimulating experience closer to those associated with PCP and PCP analogues.

## Classification
- **Categories:** dissociative, research-chemical, habit-forming
- **Also known as:** methoxmetamine

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 40-60mg
- **Light:** 20-40mg
- **Strong:** 60-100mg+
- **Threshold:** 10-20mg

*Note:  NOTE: These dosages are extremely tentative.*

## Duration
- **Duration:** 3-5 hours
- **After Effects:** 2-4 hours

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** CNS depressants.
