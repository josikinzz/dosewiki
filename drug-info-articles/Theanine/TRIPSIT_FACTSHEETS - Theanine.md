# L-Theanine
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

An amino acid that is structural similar to Glutamine. Subjective reports indicate it as a relaxing agent without much sedation. The drug is most commonly found tea, and often mixed with caffeine.

## Classification
- **Categories:** common, supplement
- **Also known as:** theanine

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 100-200mg.

## Duration
- **Onset:** 10-30 minutes
- **Duration:** 3-6 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-2 hours
