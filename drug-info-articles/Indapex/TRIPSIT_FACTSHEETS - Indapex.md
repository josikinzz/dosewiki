# Indapex
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

5-MEO-TMT is one of Alexander Shulgin's lesser known discoveries. A psychedelic tryptamine with very limited information on its effects on humans.

## Classification
- **Categories:** psychedelic, research-chemical, tentative
- **Also known as:** 5-meo-tmt

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 75-100mg
- **Light:** 60-75mg
- **Strong:** 100-150mg.

*Note:  Note: Very atypical dosing curve, caution is advised.*

## Duration
- **Onset:** 20-50 minutes
- **Duration:** 5-10 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-8 hours

## Effects
- Euphoria
- empathy
- insight
- brightened colour
- Closed/Open eye visuals
- enhanced tactile sensation
- mental/physical stimulation
- decreased appetite
- pupil dilation
- restlessness
- change in perception
- ego softening
- sweating/chills
- muscle tension
- confusion
- insomnia
