# 5-MeO-MALT
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A new and uncommon psychedelic tryptamine related to 5-MeO-DALT. Little is known about the drug, however its effects are likely similar to other psychedelic tryptamines.

## Classification
- **Categories:** psychedelic, research-chemical

## Dosage

### Insufflated
- **Common:** 10-20mg
- **Light:** 5-10mg
- **Strong:** 20mg+

### Oral
- **Common:** 25-50mg
- **Light:** 15-25mg
- **Strong:** 50mg+

### Vapourized
- **Common:** 10-15mg
- **Light:** 5-10mg
- **Strong:** 15-20mg

*Note:  NOTE: There are inconsistent reports on the drugs effects. It is recommended to start low and raise dosages slowly.*

## Duration
- **Onset:** minutes
- **Duration:** hours
