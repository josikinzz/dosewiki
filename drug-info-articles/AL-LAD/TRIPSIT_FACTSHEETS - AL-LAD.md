# AL-LAD
*Source: https://tripbot.tripsit.me/api/tripsit/getDrug/al-lad*

## Classification
- **Categories:** psychedelic, research-chemical
- **Also known as:** allad

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 75-175ug
- **Heavy:** 250ug+
- **Light:** 40-75ug
- **Strong:** 175-250ug

## Duration
- **Onset:** 45-120 minutes
- **Duration:** 4-6 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-24 hours

## Effects
- Similar in effect to LSD
- open and closed eye visuals are common
- Auditory hallucinations are reported as common
- A sense of connectedness with people and the environment around you
- A strong sense of wellbeing
