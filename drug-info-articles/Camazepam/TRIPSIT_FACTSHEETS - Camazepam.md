# Camazepam
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A benzodiazepine drug that is stronger in terms of anxiety relief than it is in terms of how hypnotic it is. It is a metabolite of diazepam and has limited anticonvulsant properties.

## Classification
- **Categories:** benzodiazepine, habit-forming, tentative, depressant
- **Also known as:** albego, limpidon, paxor

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 20-40mg
- **Heavy:** 40+
- **Light:** 10-20mg

## Duration
- **Onset:** 45-90 minutes
- **Duration:** 8-12 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-12 hours

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** Avoid all other CNS depressants.
