# 4-MPM
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

4-Methylphenmetrazine is an empathogen of the phenylmorpholine class. Reports indicate tolerance for the drug builds fast.

## Classification
- **Categories:** stimulant, research-chemical, tentative, habit-forming, empathogen
- **Also known as:** 4-mpm, 4-methylphenmetrazine

## Dosage

### Insufflated
- **Common:** 10-20mg
- **Light:** 5-10mg
- **Strong:** 20-25mg

### Oral
- **Common:** 15-25mg
- **Light:** 5-15mg
- **Strong:** 25-40mg.

## Duration
- **Onset:** minutes
- **Duration:** hours
- **After Effects:** 1-8 hours

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** Other stimulants.
