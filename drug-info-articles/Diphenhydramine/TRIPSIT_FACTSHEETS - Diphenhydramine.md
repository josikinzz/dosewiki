# Diphenhydramine
*Source: https://tripbot.tripsit.me/api/tripsit/getDrug/diphenhydramine*

## Classification
- **Categories:** depressant, deliriant, common
- **Also known as:** diphenhydramine, dph, benadryl

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 200-500mg
- **Light:** 100-250mg
- **Strong:** 450-700mg

## Duration
- **Onset:** 60-120 minutes
- **Duration:** 6-8 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-12 hours

## Effects
- Delirium
- dream-like experiences
- paranoia
- visual and audio distortions
- dry mouth
- muscle cramps
- nausea
- dysphoria
- tremors
- Often described as a pretty negative experience

## Interactions

### ⚠️ Dangerous
- **ghb/gbl**: Both substances are CNS depressants, potentiating each other strongly and unpredictably, raising the chances of memory loss, severe ataxia, and unconsciousness. The interaction will cause greater inhibition and risk of injury. If the user falls unconscious it is strongly recommended to put them into the recovery position and monitor breathing.
