# Methamnetamine
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A selective serotonin releaser, which also shows some light psychedelic activity. Almost always taken with a stimulant, to cause euphoric effects.

## Classification
- **Categories:** psychedelic, stimulant, research-chemical, tentative, empathogen, habit-forming
- **Also known as:** methylnaphetamine, mnt, n-methyl-pal-287

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 80-120mg
- **Heavy:** 150mg+
- **Light:** 60-80mg
- **Strong:** 120-150mg

## Duration
- **Onset:** 10-30 minutes
- **Duration:** 3-6 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-6 hours
