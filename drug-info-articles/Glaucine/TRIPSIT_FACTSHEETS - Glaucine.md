# Glaucine
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

An alkaloid that is found in several different scpecies in the Papaveraceae family. It has anti inflammatory and antitussive effects.

## Classification
- **Categories:** opioid, habit-forming, depressant, tentative

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 100-150mg
- **Light:** 50-100mg
- **Strong:** 150-200mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** 45-90 minutes
- **Duration:** 6-8 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-8 hours
