# GABA
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Gamma-Aminobutyric Acid. The supplement is often used for its calming effects. There is conflicting research if the drug crosses the blood brain barrier.

## Classification
- **Categories:** depressant, supplement

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 400-800mg
- **Light:** 200-400mg
- **Strong:** 800-1600mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** minutes
- **Duration:** hours
- **After Effects:** 1-2 hours
