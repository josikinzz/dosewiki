# Phentermine
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Psychostimulant of the substituted amphetamine class. The drug is used medically as an appetite suppressant.

## Classification
- **Categories:** stimulant, tentative

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 20-40mg
- **Light:** 15-20mg
- **Strong:** 40-70mg

## Duration
- **Onset:** 4-6 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-10 hours
