# Desmethylflunitrazepam
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Benzodiazepine that is a metabolite of Flunitrazepam, and has been sold as a research chemical.

## Classification
- **Categories:** depressant, benzodiazepine, habit-forming, research-chemical
- **Also known as:** fonazepam, ro05-4435, norflunitrazepam

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 1-2mg
- **Light:** 0.5-1mg
- **Strong:** 2-4mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** 30-60 minutes
- **Duration:** 5-10 hours
- **After Effects:** 2-12 hours

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** All other CNS depressants.
