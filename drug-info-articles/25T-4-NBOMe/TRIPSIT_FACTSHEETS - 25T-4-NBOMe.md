# 25T-4-NBOMe
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A psychedelic RC of the NBOMe class of drugs that is expected to cause hallucinations. Limited human use has been recorded on this drug and caution should be used with dosing.

## Classification
- **Categories:** tentative, psychedelic, research-chemical
- **Also known as:** 25t4-nbome

## Dosage

### Sublingual
- **Common:** 300-750ug
- **Light:** 150-300ug
- **Strong:** 750-1200ug.

## Duration
- **Onset:** 20-45 minutes
- **Duration:** 5-9 hours
- **After Effects:** 2-12 hours
