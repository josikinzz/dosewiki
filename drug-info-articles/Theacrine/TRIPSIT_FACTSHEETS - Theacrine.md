# Theacrine
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Small alkaloid which can be seen as a structurally modified version of caffeine, with similarly stimulating effects. The drug also shares anti-inflammatory and analgesic effects with caffeine.

## Classification
- **Categories:** stimulant, nootropic

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 25-50mg
- **Light:** 10-25mg
- **Strong:** 50-100mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** 15-30 minutes
- **Duration:** 2-6 hours
