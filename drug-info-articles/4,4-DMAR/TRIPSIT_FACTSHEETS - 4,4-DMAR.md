# 4,4-DMAR
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Very closely related stimulant to Aminorex and Pemoline. The drug has been sold under the brand name "Serotoni" and has been linked to at least 31 deaths. The drug is a potent SNDRA.

## Classification
- **Categories:** stimulant, empathogen, habit-forming, tentative
- **Also known as:** serotoni, 4,4-dmap

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 60-120mg
- **Light:** 30-60mg
- **Strong:** 120-200mg.

## Duration
- **Onset:** minutes
- **After Effects:** 1-12 hours
