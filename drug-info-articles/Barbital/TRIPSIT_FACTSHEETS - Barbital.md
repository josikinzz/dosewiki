# Barbital
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

The first commercially available barbiturate. Was used commonly as a sleeping aid until the 1950s.

## Classification
- **Categories:** barbiturate, habit-forming, depressant

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 650-970mg.

## Duration
- **Onset:** 15-30 minutes
- **Duration:** 6-12 hours

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** All other CNS depressants.
