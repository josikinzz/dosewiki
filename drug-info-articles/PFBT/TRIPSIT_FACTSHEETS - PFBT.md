# PFBT
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Tropane derivative substance that is suggested to have around ~30% the potency of cocaine as a stimulant, while also having properties of a local anasthetic.

## Classification
- **Categories:** stimulant, habit-forming, tentative, research-chemical
- **Also known as:** 4-fluorotropacocaine

## Dosage

### Insufflated
- **Common:** 50-70mg
- **Light:** 20-50mg
- **Strong:** 70-110mg+
- **Threshold:** 10-20mg

## Duration
- **Onset:** minutes
- **Duration:** hours
- **After Effects:** 1-24 hours
