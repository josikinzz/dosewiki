# 5F-AKB48
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A synthetic cannabanoid that is a Indazole. Produces subjective effects somewhat similar to that of Cannabis, yet with a very short duration. Analogue of STS-135, in which the core indole structure is subbed with an indazole base.

## Classification
- **Categories:** research-chemical, tentative, habit-forming
- **Also known as:** 5f-apinaca, 5fapinaca, 5fakb48

## Dosage

### Vapourized
- **Light:** 500-1000ug
- **Strong:** 1000-2500ug.
- **Threshold:** 500ug

## Duration

- **Duration:** minutes
- **After Effects:** minutes
