# Norflurazepam
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Benzodiazepine analogue that is an active metabolite to many other benzodiazepines, such as flurazepam, flutoprazepam, and midazolam.

## Classification
- **Categories:** benzodiazepine, depressant, tentative, habit-forming
- **Also known as:** n-desalkylflurazepam

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 5-10mg
- **Light:** 2-5mg
- **Strong:** 10-20mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** 45-120 minutes
- **Duration:** 10-16 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-12 hours

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** All other CNS depressants.
