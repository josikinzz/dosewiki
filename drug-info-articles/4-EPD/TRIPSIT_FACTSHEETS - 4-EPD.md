# 4-EPD
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

An obscure analogue of Pentedrone, there is very little information on this chemical. It is likely is shares the same general effects of the other Pentedrone family.

## Classification
- **Categories:** stimulant, habit-forming, tentative
- **Also known as:** 4-ethylpentedrone

## Dosage

### Insufflated
- **Common:** 20-30mg
- **Light:** 10-20mg
- **Strong:** 30-50mg

## Duration
- **Onset:** minutes
- **Duration:** 1-3 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-6 hours
