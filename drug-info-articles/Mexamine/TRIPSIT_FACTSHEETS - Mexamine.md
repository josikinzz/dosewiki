# Mexamine
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

5-Methoxytryptamine, a tryptamine derivative that naturally occurs in the body at low levels. There are no reports of recreational use. The drug is reported anectodally to enhances dreams.

## Classification
- **Categories:** psychedelic, tentative

## Effects
- Euphoria
- empathy
- insight
- brightened colour
- Closed/Open eye visuals
- enhanced tactile sensation
- mental/physical stimulation
- decreased appetite
- pupil dilation
- restlessness
- change in perception
- ego softening
- sweating/chills
- muscle tension
- confusion
- insomnia
