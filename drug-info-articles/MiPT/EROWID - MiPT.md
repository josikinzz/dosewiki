**U.S. FEDERAL LEGAL SUMMARY**
**MIPT**
- REGULATED: No
- STATUS: Not Approved ForHuman Consumption
- SCHEDULE: Un-Scheduled
 
MIPT is uncontrolled in the United States. This means it is generally considered legal to buy, possess, and distribute (sell, trade or give). It is possible that possession and sales could be prosecuted under the Federal Analog Act though we are unaware of any such existing cases. (8/23/04)
 
INTERNATIONAL LAW #
 
Japan #
 
MIPT is controlled as a "Designated Substance" (Shitei-Yakubutsu) by the Pharmaceutical Affairs Law, making it illegal to possess or sell (see [Ref](https://erowid.org/references/refs_view.php?ID=7395) ).
