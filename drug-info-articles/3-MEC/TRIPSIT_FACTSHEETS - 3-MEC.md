# 3-MEC
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Another cathinone based stimulant that is weaker than 4-MEC. There are few reports on this drug and it is said to be unpopular compared to alternatives.

## Classification
- **Categories:** stimulant, research-chemical, tentative, habit-forming

## Dosage

### Insufflated
- **Common:** 50-100mg
- **Light:** 25-50mg
- **Strong:** 100-150mg+

### Oral
- **Common:** 100-175mg
- **Light:** 50-100mg
- **Strong:** 175-250mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** minutes
- **Duration:** hours
- **After Effects:** hours
