# α-PBP
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Stimulant that is the homologue between α-PPP and α-PVP.

## Classification
- **Categories:** stimulant, research-chemical, habit-forming, tentative
- **Also known as:** a-pbp, alpha-pbp

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 25-50mg
- **Light:** 10-25mg
- **Strong:** 50mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** 20-35 minutes
- **Duration:** 3-4 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-24 hours
