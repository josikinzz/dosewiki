**U.S. FEDERAL LEGAL SUMMARY**
**4-Acetoxy-DET**
- REGULATED: No
- STATUS: Not Approved ForHuman Consumption
- SCHEDULE: Un-Scheduled
 
4-Acetoxy-DET and 4-HO-DET are both uncontrolled substances in the U.S. as well as in most if not all other countries. It could conceivably be covered under the analog act depending on how it's sold, but there have been no cases prosecuted that we are aware of. (1/13/2002)>
