**U.S. FEDERAL LEGAL SUMMARY**
**Naloxone**
- REGULATED: Yes
- STATUS: Prescription Only
- SCHEDULE: Un-Scheduled
 
Naloxone is unscheduled in the United States, but is available by prescription only. This means that sales and distribution are allowed only by those with a license and only to those with a prescription (according to FDA regulations). Possession is not illegal even without a prescription. 
 
 Suboxone, which contains buprenorphine HCL and naloxone HCL dihydrate, is listed as Schedule III under the Controlled Substances Act.
