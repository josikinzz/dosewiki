# Brotizolam
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Benzodiazepine analogue which is sedating, hypnotic and anxiolytic. It is available as prescription medicine in much of Europe, and is an extremely potent drug active at only 80ug. May cause amnesia and lowered inhibitions in overdose. Danger of respiratory depression when combined with other depressants. Short half-life.

## Classification
- **Categories:** depressant, benzodiazepine, habit-forming, depressant
- **Also known as:** lendormin

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 200-400ug
- **Light:** 100ug-200ug
- **Strong:** 400ug-600ug+
- **Threshold:** 80ug

## Duration
- **Onset:** minutes
- **Duration:** hours
- **After Effects:** 1-4 hours

## Effects
- Anxiolytic
- Sedative
- Muscle Relaxant
- Amnesia
- Dystaxia

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** All other CNS depressants.
