# Trazodone
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A novel antidepressant of the SARI class, a serotonin antagonist and reuptake inhibitor. The drug is used medically to treat depressive and anxiety disorders and symptoms. It is reported to have anxiolytic and hypnotic effects.

## Classification
- **Categories:** depressant

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 50-100mg
- **Light:** 25-50mg
- **Strong:** 100-150mg
