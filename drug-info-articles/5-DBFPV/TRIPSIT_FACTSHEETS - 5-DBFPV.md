# 5-DBFPV
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Stimulant of the cathinone class. Analogue of MDPV where the methylenedioxyphenyl group was replaced with a dihydrobenzofuran.

## Classification
- **Categories:** tentative, stimulant, habit-forming, research-chemical

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 15-30mg
- **Light:** 5-15mg
- **Strong:** 30-60mg.
- **Threshold:** 2-5mg

## Duration
- **Onset:** minutes
- **After Effects:** 4-12 hours
