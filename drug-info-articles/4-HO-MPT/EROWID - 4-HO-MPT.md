**U.S. FEDERAL LEGAL SUMMARY**
**4-HO-MPT**
- REGULATED: No
- STATUS: Not Approved ForHuman Consumption
- SCHEDULE: Un-Scheduled
 
4-HO-MPT is uncontrolled in the United States. It could conceivably be covered under the [U.S. Analogue Act](https://erowid.org/psychoactives/law/law_fed_analog_act) depending on how it's sold, but there have been no cases brought to trial that we are aware of. (Aug 20 2012)
 
U.S. STATE LAW #
 
Minnesota #
 
Effective August 1, 2012, Minnesota passed a law adding additional research chemicals to Schedule I. The banned compounds include 4-HO-MPT. See [http://wdoc.house.leg.state.mn.us/leg/LS87/HF2508.0.pdf](http://wdoc.house.leg.state.mn.us/leg/LS87/HF2508.0.pdf) . (thanks DW) (last updated Aug 20 2012)
