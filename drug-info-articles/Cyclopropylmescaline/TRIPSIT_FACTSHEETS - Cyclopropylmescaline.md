# Cyclopropylmescaline
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Cyclopropylmescaline, AKA CPM, is a psychedelic/hallucinogenic that was produced by Alexander Shulgin. It causes visual and auditory hallucinations and is reported to have a long duration. There is limited information on this drug, caution is advised

## Classification
- **Categories:** psychedelic, tentative, research-chemical
- **Also known as:** cpm, 4-cyclopropylmethoxy-3

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 60-70mg
- **Light:** 50-60mg
- **Strong:** 70-80mg

## Duration
- **Onset:** 10-30 minutes
- **Duration:** 12-18 hours
- **After Effects:** 6-8 hours
