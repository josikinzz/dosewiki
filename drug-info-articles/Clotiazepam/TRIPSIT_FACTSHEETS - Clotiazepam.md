# Clotiazepam
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Is a thienodiazepine substance. It differs from most other benzodiazepines in which the benzene ring has been replaced by a thiophene ring.

## Classification
- **Categories:** benzodiazepine, habit-forming, tentative, depressant
- **Also known as:** clozan, distensan, trecalmo, rize, rizen, veratran

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 5-10mg
- **Light:** 2.5-5mg
- **Strong:** 10-15mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** 10-30 minutes
- **Duration:** 4-8 hours
- **After Effects:** 4-8 hours
