# Adinazolam
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Benzodiazepine derivative that has some antidepressant properties. has been sold as a research chemical.

## Classification
- **Categories:** depressant, habit-forming, tentative
- **Also known as:** deracyn

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 15-30mg
- **Light:** 5-15mg
- **Strong:** 30-50mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** 10-25 minutes
- **Duration:** 2-5 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-16 hours

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** All other CNS depressants.
