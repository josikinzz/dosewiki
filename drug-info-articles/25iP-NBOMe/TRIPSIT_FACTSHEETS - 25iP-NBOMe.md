# 25iP-NBOMe
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Almost unheard-of analogue of 2C-iP. Extremely potent psychedelic with stimulating qualities. May cause potentially dangerous vasoconstriction at high doses. May induce uncomfortable body load.

## Classification
- **Categories:** psychedelic, research-chemical, tentative
- **Also known as:** 25ip

## Dosage

### Sublingual
- **Common:** 750-1000ug
- **Light:** 300-750ug
- **Strong:** 1000-1500ug
- **Threshold:** 250ug

## Duration
- **Onset:** 30-60 minutes
- **Duration:** 5-7 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-12 hours

## Effects
- Euphoria
- empathy
- insight
- brightened colour
- Closed/Open eye visuals
- enhanced tactile sensation
- mental/physical stimulation
- decreased appetite
- pupil dilation
- restlessness
- change in perception
- ego softening
- sweating/chills
- muscle tension
- confusion
- insomnia
