# Estazolam
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A rarely prescribed medium-duration prescription benzodiazepine. Mainly used as a hypnotic, it can cause amnesia and lowered inhibitions in higher dosages.

## Classification
- **Categories:** benzodiazepine, habit-forming, depressant
- **Also known as:** prosom, eurodin, elprazolam

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 1-2mg
- **Light:** 0.5-1mg
- **Strong:** 2-4mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** 30-60 minutes
- **Duration:** 7-12 hours
- **After Effects:** 2-24 hours

## Effects
- Anxiolytic
- Sedative
- Muscle Relaxant
- Amnesia
- Dystaxia
- Hypnotic

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** All other CNS depressants.
