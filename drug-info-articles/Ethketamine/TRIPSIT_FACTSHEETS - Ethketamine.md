# Ethketamine
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Ethketamine is a research chemical with properties similar to ketamine. While ketamine is a dissociative anesthetic, producing hallucinogenic and sedative effects, recreational users have reported euphoria and dissociation when using NENK.

## Classification
- **Categories:** dissociative, research-chemical, habit-forming
- **Also known as:** n-ethyl-norketamine, nek, n-ethylnorketamine

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 40-90mg
- **Light:** 15-40mg
- **Strong:** 90-160mg.

## Duration
- **Onset:** minutes
- **Duration:** hours
- **After Effects:** hours
