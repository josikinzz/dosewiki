# Aleph
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

The first sulphur-containing phenethylamine to have been evaluated for CNS activity for stimulant or psychedelic purposes.

## Classification
- **Categories:** psychedelic, empathogen, research-chemical, tentative
- **Also known as:** dot, para-dot

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 4-6mg
- **Light:** 2-4mg
- **Strong:** 6-12mg.

## Duration
- **Onset:** 30-90 minutes
- **Duration:** 5-10 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-24 hours
