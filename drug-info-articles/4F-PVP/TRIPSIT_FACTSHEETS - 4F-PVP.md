# 4F-PVP
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Fluorinated analogue of a-PVP. Reports indicate it to be slightly stronger than its parent compound.

## Classification
- **Categories:** tentative, stimulant, habit-forming
- **Also known as:** 4f-a-pvp, pfpvp

## Dosage

### Insufflated
- **Common:** 10-20mg
- **Light:** 5-10mg
- **Strong:** 20-25mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** minutes
- **Duration:** hours
