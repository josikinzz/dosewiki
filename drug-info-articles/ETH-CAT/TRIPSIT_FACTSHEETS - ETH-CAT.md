# Ethylcathinone
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Ethylcathinone is a synthetic stimulant. It has similar effects as MDMA in regards to euphoric effects, feelings of empathy and openness, and desire to talk with others. Similarly, it has negative effects that are associated with MDMA, such as insomnia, tightened jaw muscles, and grinding of the teeth.

## Classification
- **Categories:** stimulant, psychedelic, research-chemical, habit-forming
- **Also known as:** ethcathinone, e-cat, eth-cat
