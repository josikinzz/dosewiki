# Cyclo-Methiodrone
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Empathogen with strong stimulant effects, very loosely related to benzylpiperidine.

## Classification
- **Categories:** empathogen, stimulant, habit-forming, research-chemical, tentative

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 45-65mg
- **Heavy:** 90+mg.
- **Light:** 30-45mg
- **Strong:** 65-90mg

## Duration
- **Onset:** 25-45 minutes
- **Duration:** 3-5 hours
