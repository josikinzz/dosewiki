# Pemoline
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A stimulant of the 4-oxazolidinone class. The drug was used as a medication for ADHD and narcolepsy, but it was pulled from most markets due to liver failures among children.

## Classification
- **Categories:** stimulant, habit-forming

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 20-40mg
- **Light:** 10-20mg
- **Strong:** 40-80mg.

## Duration
- **Onset:** 15-30 minutes
- **Duration:** 4-6 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-6 hours
