# Flutoprazepam
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A potent benzodiazepine that is roughly 4-5x the potency of diazepam and has a longer duration. The drug is rarely seen outside of Japan. The drug is reported to have sedative, hypnotic and anxiolytic effects. The drug may cause amnesia and loss of inhibitions in excess. It is not recommended to mix with other depressants.

## Classification
- **Categories:** benzodiazepine, habit-forming, depressant
- **Also known as:** restas

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 1.5-3mg
- **Light:** 0.5-1mg
- **Strong:** 3-5mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** 15-45 minutes
- **Duration:** 12-16 hours
- **After Effects:** 24-36 hours

## Effects
- Anxiolytic
- Sedative
- Muscle Relaxant
- Amnesia
- Dystaxia
- Hypnotic

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** All other CNS depressants.
