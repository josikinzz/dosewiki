# 6-EAPB
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A rare entactogen benzofuran structurally related to MDMA and 6-APB. It is reported to be weaker in effects. Pharmacological knowledge on this drug is limited. The drug is suspected to be a monoamine releaser, monoamine reuptake inhibitor or both.

## Classification
- **Categories:** stimulant, psychedelic, research-chemical, empathogen, habit-forming

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 75-100mg
- **Light:** 50-75mg
- **Strong:** 100-150mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** 20-40 minutes
- **Duration:** 4-7 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-10 hours

## Effects
- Euphoria
- increased energy/alertness
- Decreased need for sleep
- Increased sociability
- mood lift
- increased libido
- Excessive talking
- Decreased appetite
- Weight loss
- Sweating
- Disturbed sleep patterns
- Bruxia
- Visual and auditory hallucinations
- itchiness
- aggressiveness
- moodiness
