# Meclonazepam
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A benzodiazepine related to clonazepam discovered by Hoffman-LaRoche in the 1960s. The drug is suspected to be useful in treating schistosomiasis. The drug has been sold on the grey market as a recreational drug but has not seen widespread popularity.

## Classification
- **Categories:** benzodiazepine, habit-forming, depressant, tentative

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 3-6mg
- **Heavy:** 6-12mg

## Duration
- **Onset:** 20-60 minutes
- **Duration:** 9-15 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-6 hours

## Effects
- Anxiolytic
- Sedative
- Muscle Relaxant
- Amnesia
- Dystaxia
- Hypnotic

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** All other CNS depressants.
