# YERBA-MATE
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

The drug is marketed for its ability to suppress appetite and burn fat using higher dosages. The drug has a high ratio of caffeine and is reported to have anti-inflammatory properties.

## Classification
- **Categories:** common, stimulant, habit-forming, nootropic

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 1000-1500mg

## Duration
- **Onset:** 10-30 minutes
- **Duration:** 4-6 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-4 hours
