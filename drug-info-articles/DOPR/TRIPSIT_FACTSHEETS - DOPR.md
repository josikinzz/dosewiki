# DOPR
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A rare psychedelic amphetamine of the same class as DOM. This is a powerful potent psychedelic with all the effects you would expect from an amphetamine. Described by Shulgin as a 'heavy duty psychedelic'. Analogue of 2C-P.

## Classification
- **Categories:** psychedelic, research-chemical, tentative

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 2.5-3.5mg
- **Heavy:** 3.5-5mg.
- **Light:** 2.5mg
- **Threshold:** 1.25mg

## Duration
- **Onset:** 90-240 minutes
- **Duration:** 18-30 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-24 hours

## Effects
- Euphoria
- empathy
- insight
- brightened colour
- Closed/Open eye visuals
- enhanced tactile sensation
- mental/physical stimulation
- decreased appetite
- pupil dilation
- restlessness
- change in perception
- ego softening
- sweating/chills
- muscle tension
- confusion
- insomnia

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** Redosing/double dosing the drug. It is recommended to avoid all other amphetamines.
