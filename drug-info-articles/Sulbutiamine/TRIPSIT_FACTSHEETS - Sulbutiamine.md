# Sulbutiamine
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A thiamine derivative nootropic and stimulant drug. Caution should be used as sulbutiamine reduces dopamine output over time with consistant usage.

## Classification
- **Categories:** tentative, stimulant, habit-forming, supplement
- **Also known as:** sulbut, arcalion, enerion, bisibuthiamine, youvitan

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 200-400mg.

## Duration
- **Onset:** 20-40 minutes
- **Duration:** 6-10 hours
