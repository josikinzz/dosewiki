# Ephenidine
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A dissociative drug which is less potent and less confusing than MXE but with otherwise similar properties. Reported to cause light stimulant effects at lower doses.

## Classification
- **Categories:** dissociative, research-chemical, habit-forming, tentative

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 75-110mg
- **Heavy:** 150mg+
- **Light:** 50-75mg
- **Strong:** 110-150mg

## Duration
- **Onset:** 10-30 minutes
- **Duration:** 3-6 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-6 hours
