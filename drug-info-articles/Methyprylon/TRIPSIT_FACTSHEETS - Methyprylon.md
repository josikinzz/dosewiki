# Methyprylon
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Sedative of the pipeidinedone family. The drug was used for treating insomnia, but is rarely used today due to alternatives with fewer side effects, such as benzodiazepines.

## Classification
- **Categories:** depressant, habit-forming, tentative
- **Also known as:** noludar

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 200-300mg
- **Light:** 150-200mg
- **Strong:** 300mg+

## Duration
- **Duration:** 6-12 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-16 hours

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** All other CNS depressants.
