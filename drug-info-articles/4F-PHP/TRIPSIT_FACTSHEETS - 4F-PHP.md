# 4F-PHP
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

4-Fluoro analogue of a-PHP. There are some reports indicating it has less manic effects than a-PHP, however the dosages seem similar.

## Classification
- **Categories:** stimulant, research-chemical, tentative, habit-forming

## Dosage

### Insufflated
- **Common:** 15-30mg
- **Light:** 5-15mg
- **Strong:** 30-50mg+

## Duration
- **Duration:** 3-6 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-24 hours
