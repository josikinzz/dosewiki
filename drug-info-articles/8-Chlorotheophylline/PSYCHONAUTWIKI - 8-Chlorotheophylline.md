# 8-Chlorotheophylline
*Source: https://psychonautwiki.org/wiki/8-Chlorotheophylline*

**8-Chlorotheophylline** ( **1,3-dimethyl-8-chloroxanthine** or **8-CTP** ) is a stimulant drug of the [xanthine](https://psychonautwiki.org/wiki/Xanthine) class that is closely related to [caffeine](https://psychonautwiki.org/wiki/Caffeine) in both its chemical structure and effects. It is mainly used as a [wakefulness](https://psychonautwiki.org/wiki/Wakefulness) -promoting agent found within [dimenhydrinate](https://psychonautwiki.org/wiki/Dimenhydrinate) , where it counteracts the [drowsiness](https://psychonautwiki.org/wiki/Sleepiness) effects present with [diphenhydramine](https://psychonautwiki.org/wiki/Diphenhydramine) . **8-Chlorotheophylline** can cause heart problems in people who take large doses of dimenhydrinate as both of the active ingredients in the drug, Diphenhydramine, and **8-Chlorotheophylline** together can cause severe tachycardia.
