# Isopropylphenidate
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

IPPH is a novel piperidine based methylphenidate analogue and NDRI stimulant. It is reported to have less physical side effects.

## Classification
- **Categories:** research-chemical, habit-forming, tentative, stimulant
- **Also known as:** iph, ipph, ippd, ipp

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 10-15mg
- **Light:** 7-10mg
- **Strong:** 15-30mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** 30 minutes
- **Duration:** 3-6 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-8 hours
