# Naphyrone
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A drug derived from pyrovalerone which acts as a TRI, producing stimulating effects. The drug has been sold in a blend called "NRG-1". There is limited information on the drug's safety profile.

## Classification
- **Categories:** stimulant, research-chemical, habit-forming

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 20-35mg
- **Strong:** 35-50mg+

## Duration
- **Duration:** 6-12 hours
