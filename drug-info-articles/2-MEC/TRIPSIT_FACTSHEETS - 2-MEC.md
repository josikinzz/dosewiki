# 2-MEC
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Cathinone which some users report to feel like a weaker MDMA with a shorter onset.

## Classification
- **Categories:** stimulant, research-chemical, habit-forming, tentative

## Dosage

### Insufflated
- **Common:** 50-100mg
- **Light:** 25-50mg
- **Strong:** 100-250mg+

### Oral
- **Common:** 100-200mg
- **Light:** 50-100mg
- **Strong:** 200-350mg+

## Duration
- **Duration:** hours
- **After Effects:** 1-6 hours
