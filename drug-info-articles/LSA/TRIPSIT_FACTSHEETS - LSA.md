# LSA
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A chemical found in Morning Glory and Hawaiian Baby Woodrose seeds, which are often sold legally in various stores. The drug is reported to have mental effects similar to LSD, although reportedly little to no visual effects. The drug is reported to frequently cause nausea and causing excessive time dilation at higher dosages.

## Classification
- **Categories:** psychedelic, common
- **Also known as:** hbwr, morningglory, hbmg, Morning, ololiuqui, morning_glory, hbw

## Dosage

### Hbwr
- **Common:** 2-6seeds
- **Heavy:** 14seeds+
- **Light:** 1-2seeds
- **Strong:** 6-14seeds

### Morning_Glory
- **Common:** 100-250seeds
- **Heavy:** 400seeds+
- **Light:** 50-100seeds
- **Strong:** 250-400seeds

*Note: Some individuals report a hangover, characterized by a feelings of mental sluggishness and dulled emotions.*

## Duration
- **Onset:** 30-180 minutes
- **Duration:** 4-10 hours
