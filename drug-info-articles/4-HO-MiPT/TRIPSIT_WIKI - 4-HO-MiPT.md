# 4-HO-MiPT

*Source: https://wiki.tripsit.me/wiki/4-HO-MiPT*

A Psilocin analogue, 4-HO-MiPT is similar to shrooms with a slightly shorter duration. A rather visual substance, it lends itself to lighter trips. 

## Dosage

Like other similar substances, this seems to be physically well tolerated at higher doses.

| Threshold | 5-10mg |
| --- | --- |
| Light | 10-15mg |
| Common | 15-20mg |
| Strong | 20-30mg |
| Heavy | 40+mg |

## Duration

| Onset | 20-60 minutes |
| --- | --- |
| Total | 4-6 hours |
| After-effects | 2-4 hours |

## Effects

### Postive

- Euphoria
- Sense of wonder or connected-ness
- Creativity
- increased Music appreciation
- Hallucinations
- Synaesthesia
- Traces

### Neutral

- Altered perception of time
- Altered thought processes
- Increased emotion

### Negative

- Uneasy stomach after dosing
- Strong negative feelings
- Confusion
- Paranoia
- Thought Loops

## Harm Reduction

This substance seems to be well tolerated. While structurally similar to known safe substances, it is no guaranty of safety. 

Refer to [Psychedelic Harm Reduction](https://wiki.tripsit.me/wiki/Psychedelics#Harm_Reduction) for general information.
