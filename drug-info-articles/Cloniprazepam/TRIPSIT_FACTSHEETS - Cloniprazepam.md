# Cloniprazepam
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Prodrug for Clonazepam.

## Classification
- **Categories:** benzodiazepine, depressant, habit-forming, tentative

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 1-2mg
- **Light:** .5-1mg
- **Strong:** 2-4mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** 15-45 minutes
- **Duration:** 6-9 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-8 hours

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** All other CNS depressants.
