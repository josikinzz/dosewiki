# HOT-2
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A rare psychedelic phenylethylamine that is the hydroxylated version and prodrug for 2C-T-2.

## Classification
- **Categories:** psychedelic, research-chemical, tentative
- **Also known as:** hot2

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 12-15mg
- **Light:** 10-12mg
- **Strong:** 15-20mg+
- **Threshold:** 8mg

## Duration
- **Onset:** 20-40 minutes
- **Duration:** 5-12 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-12 hours

## Effects
- Euphoria
- empathy
- insight
- brightened colour
- Closed/Open eye visuals
- enhanced tactile sensation
- mental/physical stimulation
- decreased appetite
- pupil dilation
- restlessness
- change in perception
- ego softening
- sweating/chills
- muscle tension
- confusion
- insomnia

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** The drug is reported to have traces of 2C-T-2 in imperfect synthesis. As a result, individuals ingesting HOT-2 may run the risk of additionally ingesting 2C-T-2, which notably has MAOI properties.
