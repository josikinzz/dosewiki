# YOPO
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Anadenanthera peregrina, also known as yopo, is a perennial tree native to the Caribbean and South America. It is an entheogen containing 5-MeO-DMT and DMT, and is used in healing ceremonies and rituals. The tree is also a source of dietary calcium.
