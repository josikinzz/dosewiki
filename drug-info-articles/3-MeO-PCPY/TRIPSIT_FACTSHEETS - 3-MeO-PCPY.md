# 3-MeO-PCPY
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

3-Methoxy analogue of Rolicyclidine (PCPy) and less opioid effects than Phenylcyclidine (PCP) - Yet it is still prominent.

## Classification
- **Categories:** dissociative, habit-forming, tentative, research-chemical

## Dosage

### Insufflated
- **Common:** 3-6mg
- **Light:** 1-3mg
- **Strong:** 6-10mg+

### Oral
- **Common:** 4-8mg
- **Light:** 2-4mg
- **Strong:** 8-15mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** minutes
- **Duration:** hours
- **After Effects:** 1-6 hours

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** All other CNS depressants.
