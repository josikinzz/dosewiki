**U.S. FEDERAL LEGAL SUMMARY**
**Pregabalin**
- REGULATED: Yes
- STATUS: Scheduled
- SCHEDULE: Schedule V
- CLASSIFICATION: Depressant
 
Pregabalin is Schedule V in the U.S. This means it is illegal to sell without a license and illegal to possess without a valid license or prescription.
