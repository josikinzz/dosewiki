# Mexazolam
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Benzodiazepine derivative that had been trialed for anxiety and was found to be effective for anxiety at one week week follow-ups, but after three weeks it had lost its associated therapeutic anxiolytic properties and was reported to be no more effective than the placebo.

## Classification
- **Categories:** depressant, habit-forming
- **Also known as:** melex, sedoxil

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 0.5-1.5mg
- **Heavy:** 2-3mg.
- **Light:** 0.25-0.5mg
- **Strong:** 1.5-2mg

## Duration
- **Onset:** 5-45 minutes
- **Duration:** 4-8 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-12 hours
