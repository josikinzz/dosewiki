# Allobarbital
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

It is a barbiturate that was first made in 1912. It was preliminary used as an anticonvulsant. Yet it has pretty much been outclassed by a newer generation of safer ones. Still in use in Poland.

## Classification
- **Categories:** barbiturate, tentative, depressant, habit-forming

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 1000-1500mg
- **Light:** 500-1000mg
- **Strong:** 1500-2000mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** 15-30 minutes
- **Duration:** 6-10 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-12 hours

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** All other CNS depressants.
