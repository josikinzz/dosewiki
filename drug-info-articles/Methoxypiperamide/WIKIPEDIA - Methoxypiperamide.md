# Methoxypiperamide
*Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Methoxypiperamide*

Methoxypiperamide (also known as MeOP and MEXP) is a psychoactive drug of the piperazine class that has been sold online as a designer drug. It is the 4-methoxy-Î±-keto analog of methylbenzylpiperazine.
Very little data exists about the pharmacology and toxicity of methoxypiperamide; however, the US state of Vermont classifies it as a hallucinogen.

## Legal status

Methoxypiperamide is a controlled substance in Vermont as of January 2016, and also in Hungary.
