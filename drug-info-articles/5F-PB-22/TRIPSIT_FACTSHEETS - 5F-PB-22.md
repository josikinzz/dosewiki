# 5F-PB-22
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Synthetic cannabinoid, agonist of the cannabinoid receptors which has a strong sedating aspect. The drug has a fast onset as common with synthetic cannabinoid.

## Classification
- **Categories:** tentative, research-chemical, habit-forming

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 3-5mg
- **Light:** 1-3mg
- **Strong:** 5-8mg.
- **Threshold:** 1mg

*Note:  Note: As with all research chemicals, start with a low dose, and work your way up.*

## Duration
- **Onset:** 5-10 minutes
- **Duration:** 60-90 minutes
- **After Effects:** 30-60 minutes
