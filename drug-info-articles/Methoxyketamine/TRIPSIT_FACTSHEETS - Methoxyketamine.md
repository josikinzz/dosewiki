# Methoxyketamine
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

An arylcyclohexylamine drug and analogue of ketamine. The drug is slightly less potent than ketamine. It is reported to have similar dissociative and anasthetic effects to ketamine. The drug is reported to be extremely rare.

## Classification
- **Categories:** dissociative, research-chemical, habit-forming
- **Also known as:** 2-meo-ketamine

## Dosage

### Insufflated
- **Common:** 60-130mg
- **Light:** 15-60mg
- **Strong:** 130-200mg+
- **Threshold:** 5-15mg

## Duration
- **Onset:** 10-20 minutes
- **Duration:** 1-2 hours
- **After Effects:** 2-4 hours
