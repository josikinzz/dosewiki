# Bentazepam
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Thienodiazepine that is an effective anxiolyric. Liver damage among other concerns have been reported.

## Classification
- **Categories:** benzodiazepine, depressant, habit-forming
- **Also known as:** thiadipone, tiadipona

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 30-50mg
- **Light:** 15-30mg
- **Strong:** 50-75mg+

*Note:  NOTE: Liver damage has been reported by users. If you are using this substance for a prolonged period of time it is recommended to get testing done on your liver.*

## Duration
- **Onset:** 15-45 minutes
- **Duration:** 3-6 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-8 hours

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** All other CNS depressants.
