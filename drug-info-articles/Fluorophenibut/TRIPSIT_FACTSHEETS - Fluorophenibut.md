# Fluorophenibut
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

An anxiolytic analogue of GABA and phenibut, with similar activity to the latter. Suggested to be 5-10x potent as phenibut, with some preliminary reports indicating tolerance builds more slowly.

## Classification
- **Categories:** tentative, depressant, habit-forming

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 100-150mg
- **Light:** 50-100mg
- **Strong:** 200-250mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** 60-120 minutes
- **Duration:** 8-12 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-8 hours
