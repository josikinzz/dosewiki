# DALT
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Psychedelic drug of the tryptamine class.

## Classification
- **Categories:** psychedelic, research-chemical, tentative
- **Also known as:** n,n-diallyltryptamine, diallyltryptamine

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 40-60mg
- **Light:** 20-40mg
- **Strong:** 60mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** 15-45 minutes
- **Duration:** 3-6 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-4 hours

## Effects
- Euphoria
- empathy
- insight
- brightened colour
- Closed/Open eye visuals
- enhanced tactile sensation
- mental/physical stimulation
- decreased appetite
- pupil dilation
- restlessness
- change in perception
- ego softening
- sweating/chills
- muscle tension
- confusion
- insomnia
