# Oxazepam
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A prescription benzodiazepine with intermediate duration and half life. A sedative and hypnotic which may cause lowered inhibitions and amnesia in higher doses.

## Classification
- **Categories:** benzodiazepine, depressant, habit-forming
- **Also known as:** serax

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 10-30mg
- **Light:** 5-10mg
- **Strong:** 30-45mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** 60-120 minutes
- **Duration:** 4-6 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-10 hours

## Effects
- The drug is reported to have effects associated with typical common benzodiazepines
- Amnesic
- anxiolytic
- anticonvulsant
- hypnotic
- sedative

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** All other CNS depressants.
