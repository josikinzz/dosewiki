# 4-Chlorodiazepam
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A benzodiazepine derivative of Diazepam, yet lacks affinity for GABA(a) receptors, yet is a sedative that has pro-convulsant effects. At lower doses it has been shown to be neuroprotective.

## Classification
- **Categories:** research-chemical, tentative
- **Also known as:** ro5-4864, 4'-chlorodiazepam
