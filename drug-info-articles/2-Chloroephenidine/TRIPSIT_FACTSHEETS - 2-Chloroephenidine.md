# 2-Chloroephenidine
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Analogue of Ephenidine. Very little is known about this compound. It is very likely to exhibit dissociative effects, and is slightly less potent than the parent drug Ephenidine.

## Classification
- **Categories:** dissociative, research-chemical, tentative
- **Also known as:** 2-chloroephenidine, 2chloroephenidine, 2-cl-ephenidine, 2clephenidine

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 130-175mg
- **Heavy:** 200mg+
- **Light:** 100-130mg
- **Strong:** 175-200mg

## Duration
- **Onset:** 10-30 minutes
- **Duration:** 3-6 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-6 hours
