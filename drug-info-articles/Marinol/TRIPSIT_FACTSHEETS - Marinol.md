# Marinol
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Synthetically created Δ9-THC, the main psychoactive ingredient in cannabis.

## Classification
- **Categories:** depressant, tentative
- **Also known as:** dronabinol, syndros, cesamet, δ9-thc, δ9--tetrahydrocannabinol, delta9-tetrahydrocannabinol, delta9-thc

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 20-30mg
- **Light:** 10-20mg
- **Strong:** 30-50mg+

## Duration
- **Duration:** hours
- **After Effects:** 1-4 hours

## Additional Information
- **Half-life:** 1.6–59 h,[3] 25–36 h (orally administered dronabinol)
