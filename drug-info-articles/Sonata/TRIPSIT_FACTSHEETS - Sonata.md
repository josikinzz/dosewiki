# Sonata
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Sedative-hypnotic of the nonbenzodiazepine (Z-drug) of the pyrazolopyramide class. The drug is mainly used medically for short-term insomnia.

## Classification
- **Categories:** depressant, habit-forming, common
- **Also known as:** zaleplon

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 5-10mg
- **Light:** 2.5-5mg
- **Strong:** 10-20mg.

## Duration
- **Onset:** 10-30 minutes
- **Duration:** 3-6 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-12 hours

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** All other CNS depressants.
