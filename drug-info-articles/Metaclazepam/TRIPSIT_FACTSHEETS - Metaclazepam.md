# Metaclazepam
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A benzodiazepine derivative, reports indicate anxiolytic effects while lacking hypnotic effects associated with common benzodiazepines. It has an active metabolite N-desmethylmetaclazepam which is the main metabolite of metaclazepam. The drug is indicated as slightly more effective as an anxiolytic than bromazepam.

## Classification
- **Categories:** habit-forming, tentative, depressant

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 18.75mg
- **Light:** 9.5mg-18.75mg
- **Strong:** 35-50mg+

## Duration
- **After Effects:** 1-24 hours

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** All other CNS depressants.
