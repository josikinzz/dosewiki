# Bromazolam
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A rather novel Benzodiazepine that is structurally quite close to Alprazolam.

## Classification
- **Categories:** benzodiazepine, research-chemical, tentative, habit-forming
- **Also known as:** xli-268, xli268

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 1-2mg
- **Light:** 0.5-1mg
- **Strong:** 2-4mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** 15-45 minutes
- **Duration:** 5-8 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-12 hours

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** All other CNS depressants.
