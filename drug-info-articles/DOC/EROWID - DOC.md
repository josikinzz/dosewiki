DOC Dosage
 
by Erowid
 
---
 
 
DOC is a phenethylamine found most frequently in powder form. It is generally injested orally. Following are approximate dosages for pure DOC.
 
- Oral DOC Dosages Light1.0 - 1.5 mg Common1.5 - 2.5 mg Strong2.5 - 5.0 mg Heavy5.0 + | Oral DOC Dosages | Light | 1.0 - 1.5 mg | Common | 1.5 - 2.5 mg | Strong | 2.5 - 5.0 mg | Heavy | 5.0 +
**Oral DOC Dosages**
- Light: 1.0 - 1.5 mg
- Common: 1.5 - 2.5 mg
- Strong: 2.5 - 5.0 mg
- Heavy: 5.0 +
**Onset : 0.5 - 2.5 hours Duration : 8 - 20 hours (depending on dosage) Normal After Effects : 6 - 12 hours**

U.S. FEDERAL LAW #
 
Caution : All legal information should be verified through other sources. [ see below ]
 
**U.S. FEDERAL LEGAL SUMMARY**
**DOI**
- REGULATED: No
- STATUS: Not Approved ForHuman Consumption
- SCHEDULE: Likely Analog
 
DOC is unscheduled in the United States. It is likely that it would be considered an analog (of DOB), in which case, sales for human consumption or possession with the intent to ingest could be prosecuted under the [Federal Analogue Act](https://erowid.org/psychoactives/law/analog/analog.shtml) though we are unaware of any such existing cases (3/05/06).

INTERNATIONAL LAW #
 
Australia #
 
Unknown. May fall in Schedule II with DOB, but we have been unable to confirm this
 
Canada #
 
DOC is [Schedule III in Canada](https://erowid.org/psychoactives/law/countries/law_canada.shtml) , making it illegal to sell, buy, or possess without a license. It also would be controlled under the 2016 regulation that controlled a wide variety phenethylamines, adding "2C-phenethylamines and their salts, derivatives, isomers and salts of derivatives and isomers that correspond" to a broad definition that schedules anything with a 2,5-dimethoxyphenylethamine core. This includes most 2C-s, DOx, TMA, Aleph, NBOMes, NBOHs, NBF, bk-2-C-B, 2C-B-Fly, bromo-dragonfly, etc. See [http://www.gazette.gc.ca/rp-pr/p2/2016/2016-05-04/html/sor-dors73-eng.php](http://www.gazette.gc.ca/rp-pr/p2/2016/2016-05-04/html/sor-dors73-eng.php) . (thanks r) (last updated May 29 2016)
 
China #
 
Controlled in China as a Category I psychotropic substance, illegal to sell, buy, import, export, and manufacture 4-methylmethcathinone as of September 2010. See [China's SFDA announcement](https://erowid.org/psychoactives/law/countries/china/china_law_2015_09_27_list_of_newly_controlled_chemicals.pdf) . (thanks n) (last updated Nov 25, 2015)
 
Denmark #
 
Denmark added 2C-E, 2C-P, DOC, and DOI to the list of Schedule I controlled substances as of Paril 8, 2007. See [ELDD](http://eldd.emcdda.europa.eu/index.cfm?fuseaction=public.content&sLanguageISO=EN&nNodeID=5176&pluginMethod=eldd.shownewsdetails&id=03/04/2007DENMARK:%20New%20substances%20under%20control) . (Last Updates Apr 5 2007)
 
Germany (Deutschland) #
 
DOC is listed in [Anlage I](https://erowid.org/psychoactives/law/countries/germany/germany.shtml) in Germany, making it illegal to buy, sell, or possess without a license. (thanks F)
 
New Zealand #
 
DOC is Schedule III (Class C) in New Zealand as an "amphetamine analogue" [ [reference](https://erowid.org/psychoactives/law/countries/nz/law_nz_info1.shtml#analogues-amphetamine) ].
 
Poland #
 
DOC is not controlled in Poland and no analog law covers it. See [http://talk.hyperreal.info/ustawa-przeciwdzia-aniu-narkomanii-ustawy-pokrewne-t30408.html](http://talk.hyperreal.info/ustawa-przeciwdzia-aniu-narkomanii-ustawy-pokrewne-t30408.html) and [http://isap.sejm.gov.pl/DetailsServlet?id=WDU20051791485](http://isap.sejm.gov.pl/DetailsServlet?id=WDU20051791485 ) . (unconfirmed) (thanks zv) (last updated July 23 2011)
 
U.K. #
 
DOC is [Schedule I/Class A](https://erowid.org/psychoactives/law/countries/uk/uk_law_definitions.shtml) in the U.K., making it illegal to sell, buy, or possess without a license.
