# Quazepam
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Benzodiazepine derivate drug, Doral is prescribed for short-term treatment of insomnia and sleep maintenance. It's method of action is reportedly similar to zolpedim and zaleplon (and substitutes) when tested in animal studies.

## Classification
- **Categories:** benzodiazepine, depressant, habit-forming
- **Also known as:** doral

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 10-15mg.
- **Light:** 7.5-10mg
- **Strong:** 15-30mg.

## Duration
- **Onset:** 10-30 minutes
- **Duration:** 6-12 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-24 hours
