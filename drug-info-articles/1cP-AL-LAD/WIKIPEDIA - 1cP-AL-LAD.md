# 1cP-AL-LAD
*Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/1cP-AL-LAD*

1cP-AL-LAD is a psychedelic drug of the lysergamide family related to lysergic acid diethylamide (LSD). It is thought to act as prodrug of AL-LAD.

### Analogues

Related compounds include 1cP-LSD, 1D-LSD, 1P-AL-LAD, and 1T-AL-LAD, among others.

## History

1cP-AL-LAD has been sold as a designer drug. It was first identified in France in June 2021.
