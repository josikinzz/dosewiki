# Thiopental
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Barbiturate that that has a reportedly fast onset. The drug is used as for anesthesia in medical induced comas among other things.

## Classification
- **Categories:** barbiturate, habit-forming, depressant

## Duration
- **Onset:** minutes
- **Duration:** hours
- **After Effects:** 1-24 hours

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** All other CNS depressants.
