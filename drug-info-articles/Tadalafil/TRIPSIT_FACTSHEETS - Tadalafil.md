# Tadalafil
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Tadalafil, a PDE5 inhibitor used to combat erectile disfunction. Dangerous in combination with other drugs which lower blood pressure.

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 5-10mg
- **Light:** 2.5-5mg
- **Strong:** 10-20mg.

## Duration
- **Duration:** 4-6 hours
