# Acetildenafil
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

An RC analogue of sildenafil (viagra) often missold as a hidden ingredient in many 'natural' sexual potency blends and supplements.

## Classification
- **Categories:** research-chemical

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 15-30mg
- **Light:** 5-15mg
- **Strong:** 30-40mg

## Duration
- **Onset:** 10-45 minutes
- **Duration:** 3-7 hours
