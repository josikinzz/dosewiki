# Bromadol
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A very potent opioid that has an arylcyclohexylamine structure. Intial studies estimated that it was ~10,000x Morphine in animal studies. However that value has since decreased to ~504 times the potency of morphine for the trans-isomer.

## Classification
- **Categories:** opioid, depressant, habit-forming, tentative
- **Also known as:** bdpc

## Duration
- **Onset:** minutes
- **Duration:** hours

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** All other CNS depressants.
