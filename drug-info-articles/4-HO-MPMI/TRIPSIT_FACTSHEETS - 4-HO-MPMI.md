# 4-HO-MPMI
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A rare tryptamine derivative first synthesised by David Nichols. This drug, also known as lucigenol, has rarely been documented outside the lab. It is known to be a psychedelic similar to other psychoactive tryptamines, with a potency similar to DOI. Little else is known about the drug.

## Classification
- **Categories:** psychedelic, research-chemical, tentative
- **Also known as:** lucigenol

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 1-2mg.
- **Light:** 750ug-1mg
- **Threshold:** 500-750ug.

*Note:  Note: As of writing this note, this substance has not been reported on dosages past 2mg. Caution should be given when dosing. The drug is near eqipotent to DOI.*

## Duration
- **Onset:** 15-60 minutes
- **Duration:** 6-8 hours

- empathy

- enhanced tactile sensation
- mental/physical stimulation
- decreased appetite
- pupil dilation
- restlessness

- ego softening
- sweating/chills
- muscle tension
- confusion
