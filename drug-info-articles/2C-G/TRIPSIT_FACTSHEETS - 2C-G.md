# 2C-G
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Extremely rare drug of the 2C-X family. An extremely long lasting substance. Is nearly as potent as its amphetamine counter-part, Ganesha. Yet is said to have very little visual activity.

## Classification
- **Categories:** psychedelic, research-chemical, tentative
- **Also known as:** 2cg

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 25-30mg
- **Light:** 20-25mg
- **Strong:** 30mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** 30-120 minutes
- **Duration:** 15-35 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-72 hours

## Effects
- Euphoria
- empathy
- insight
- brightened colour
- Closed/Open eye visuals
- enhanced tactile sensation
- mental/physical stimulation
- decreased appetite
- pupil dilation
- restlessness
- change in perception
- ego softening
- sweating/chills
- muscle tension
- confusion
- insomnia
