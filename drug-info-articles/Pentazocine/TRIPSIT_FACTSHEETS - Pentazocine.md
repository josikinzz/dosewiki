# Pentazocine
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A synthetic opioid used as an analgesic. It is only used IM and IV in the medical field and has respiratory depressant effects. It is pregnancy category C and schedule IV in the US.

## Classification
- **Categories:** habit-forming, depressant, opioid
- **Also known as:** talwin

## Dosage

### Intramuscular
- **Common:** 20mg-30mg
- **Light:** 10-20mg
- **Strong:** 30-60mg

### Intravenous
- **Common:** 10-20mg
- **Light:** 5-10mg
- **Strong:** 30mg

## Duration
- **Onset:** minutes
- **Duration:** hours
- **After Effects:** 1-12 hours

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** All other CNS depressants
