# Isoproscaline
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A analogue of mescaline, closely related to proscaline.

## Classification
- **Categories:** psychedelic, research-chemical, tentative
- **Also known as:** ip

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 50-60mg
- **Light:** 40-50mg
- **Strong:** 60-80mg+
- **Threshold:** 30mg

## Duration
- **Onset:** 60-180 minutes
- **Duration:** 10-20 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-12 hours

## Effects
- Euphoria
- empathy
- insight
- brightened colour
- Closed/Open eye visuals
- enhanced tactile sensation
- mental/physical stimulation
- decreased appetite
- pupil dilation
- restlessness
- change in perception
- ego softening
- sweating/chills
- muscle tension
- confusion
- insomnia
