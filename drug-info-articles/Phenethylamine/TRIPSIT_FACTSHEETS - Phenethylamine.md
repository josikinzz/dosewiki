# Phenethylamine
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Organic compound, which is the name of a class of chemicals that are known for psychoactive and/or stimulant effects. The compound is typically not used as a supplement as it is rapidly broken down into inactive compounds. The compound is found in many foods.

## Classification
- **Categories:** supplement, nootropic
- **Also known as:** pea

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 300-500mg.

## Duration
- **Onset:** 15-30 minutes
- **Duration:** 1-4 hours
- **After Effects:** 10-60 minutes
