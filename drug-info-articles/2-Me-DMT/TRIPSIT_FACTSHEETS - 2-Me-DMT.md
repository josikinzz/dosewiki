# 2-Me-DMT
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Also known as 2,N,N-TMT, this drug was first synthesised by Alexander Shulgin. Described as a very mild psychedelic of similar subjective effects to other tryptamines. Reported as having an unusual and unpredictable dose curve, it is recommended to start low with this drug.

## Classification
- **Categories:** psychedelic, research-chemical, tentative

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 50-75mg
- **Light:** 30-50mg
- **Strong:** 75-100mg

## Duration
- **Onset:** minutes
- **Duration:** 3-8 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-5 hours

## Effects
- Euphoria
- empathy
- insight
- brightened colour
- Closed/Open eye visuals
- enhanced tactile sensation
- mental/physical stimulation
- decreased appetite
- pupil dilation
- restlessness
- change in perception
- ego softening
- sweating/chills
- muscle tension
- confusion
- insomnia
