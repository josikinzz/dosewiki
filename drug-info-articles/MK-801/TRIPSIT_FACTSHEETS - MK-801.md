# MK-801
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Dissociative research chemical reported to be similar to PCP and Ketamine. Dizocilpine, also known as MK-801, is a non-competitive antagonist of the NMDA receptor.

## Classification
- **Categories:** dissociative, research-chemical, tentative, habit-forming
- **Also known as:** dizocilpine, mk801

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 50-100ug

## Duration
- **Onset:** minutes
- **Duration:** 20-36 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-72 hours

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** Unnecessary movement due to risk of injury. Mixing with other depressants such as alcohol, benzodiazepines, and opiods.
