# 4-CMC
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

This summary is written based on few sources: The cathinone analogue of 4-CMA, which is highly neurotoxic. It is not known if the cathinone family holds the same neurotoxicity concern as with the parent compound.

## Classification
- **Categories:** stimulant, research-chemical, habit-forming
- **Also known as:** 4cmc

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 50mg-100mg
- **Light:** 30-50mg
- **Strong:** 100mg+
- **Threshold:** 20-30mg

## Duration
- **Onset:** minutes
- **Duration:** hours

## Effects
- Euphoria
- increased energy/alertness
- decreased need for sleep
- increased sociability
- mood lift
- increased libido
- excessive talking
- decreased appetite
- weight loss
- sweating
- disturbed sleep patterns
- bruxism
- visual and auditory hallucinations
- itchiness
- aggressiveness
- moodiness
