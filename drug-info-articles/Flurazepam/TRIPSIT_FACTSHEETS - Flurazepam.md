# Flurazepam
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Benzodiazepine with an extremely long half-life, between 40-250 hours. The drug is typically used for general anxiety disorder, and is reported to be sedating, hypnotic, anxiolytic. The drug may cause effects of amnesia and reduced inhibitions.

## Classification
- **Categories:** benzodiazepine, habit-forming, depressant
- **Also known as:** dalmane

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 15-30mg
- **Light:** 5-10mg
- **Strong:** 30-60mg.

## Duration
- **Onset:** 45-90 minutes
- **Duration:** 12-16 hours
- **After Effects:** 2-48 hours

## Effects
- Anxiolytic
- Sedative
- Muscle Relaxant
- Amnesia
- Dystaxia
- Hypnotic

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** All other CNS depressants.
