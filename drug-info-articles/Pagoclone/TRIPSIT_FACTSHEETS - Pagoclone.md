# Pagoclone
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

An anxiolytic drug related to zopiclone with reportedly no sedative or hypnotic qualities. The drug has been investigated as a possible alcohol replacement. Related in structure and effect to zopiclone and zolpidem.

## Classification
- **Categories:** depressant, habit-forming, tentative, research-chemical
