# Cinolazepam
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A benzodiazepine derivative drug with anxiolytic effects but mainly hypnotic effects. It is extremely effective as a sleep aid but has mild euphoria compared to other benzodiazepines.

## Classification
- **Categories:** depressant, habit-forming
- **Also known as:** geroderm

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 30-60mg
- **Light:** 10-30mg
- **Strong:** 60-120mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** 30-90 minutes
- **Duration:** 8-12 hours
- **After Effects:** 12-20 hours

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** Caution: Do not mix with any CNS depressants. The combination can prove to be fatal and also increases likelihood of blacking out.
