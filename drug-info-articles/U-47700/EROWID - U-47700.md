**U.S. FEDERAL LEGAL SUMMARY**
**U-47700**
- REGULATED: Pending
- STATUS: Pending Scheduling
- SCHEDULE: Schedule I (temporary)
 
As published in the Federal Register on Sep 7, 2016, U-47700 is being temporarily placed in Schedule I the United States, likely effective Oct 7, 2016. [ [Temporary Placement of U-47700 into Schedule I](https://www.federalregister.gov/articles/2016/09/07/2016-21477/schedules-of-controlled-substances-temporary-placement-of-u-47700-into-schedule-i) ] Prior to this development it was possible that it could be considered an analog of a scheduled substance, in which case, sales for human consumption or possession with the intent to ingest could be prosecuted under the [Federal Analogue Act](https://erowid.org/psychoactives/law/analog/analog.shtml) though we are unaware of any such existing cases.
 
U.S. STATE LAW #
 
Ohio #
 
U-47700 is emergency scheduled as a Schedule I substance in Ohio. See [Ohio Executive Order 2016-01K](http://governor.ohio.gov/Portals/0/pdf/executiveOrders/Executive%20Order%202016-01K.pdf) . (thanks r) (last updated May 11 2016)
 
INTERNATIONAL LAW #
 
Sweden #
 
U-47700 was controlled in Sweden in January 2016. See [https://folkhalsomyndigheten.se/nyheter-och-press/nyhetsarkiv/2015/november/31-nya-amnen-kan-klassas-som-narkotika-eller-halsofarlig-vara/](https://folkhalsomyndigheten.se/nyheter-och-press/nyhetsarkiv/2015/november/31-nya-amnen-kan-klassas-som-narkotika-eller-halsofarlig-vara/) (last updated May 11 2016)
