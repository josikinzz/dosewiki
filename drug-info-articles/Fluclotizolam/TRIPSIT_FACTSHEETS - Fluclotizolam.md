# Fluclotizolam
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A derivative of Etizolam. There are conflicting reports on its dosage, though reports have suggested that it is approximately 3x the potency of Etizolam, with a shorter half-life.

## Classification
- **Categories:** benzodiazepine, tentative, research-chemical, habit-forming

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 250-500ug
- **Heavy:** 750ug+
- **Light:** 250ug
- **Strong:** 500-750ug

*Note:  Note: These doses are extremely tentative and will vary from user to user depending on many factors including, but not limited to, tolerance.*

## Duration
- **Onset:** 10-30 minutes
- **Duration:** 3-6 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-14 hours
