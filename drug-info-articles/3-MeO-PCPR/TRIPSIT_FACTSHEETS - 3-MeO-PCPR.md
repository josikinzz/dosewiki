# 3-MeO-PCPR
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

This is the N-Propyl homologue of 3-MeO-PCE. It is slightly less potent than PCP. Yet seems slightly less manic inducing.

## Classification
- **Categories:** dissociative, tentative, research-chemical, habit-forming

## Dosage

### Insufflated
- **Common:** 3-8mg
- **Light:** 1-3mg
- **Strong:** 8-15mg+

### Oral
- **Common:** 8-15mg
- **Light:** 5-8mg
- **Strong:** 15-20mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** minutes
- **Duration:** hours
- **After Effects:** 1-4 hours
