# Cyclobenzaprine
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Muscle relaxant and CNS depressant used to relieve skeletal muscle spasms and associated pain in acute musculoskeletal conditions. Sometimes prescribed off-label for treatment of fibromyalgia or as a sleep aid. The drug is reported to have little recreational value alone, but may potentiate some opioids. May cause drowsiness, dry mouth and dizziness.

## Classification
- **Categories:** depressant, habit-forming, common
- **Also known as:** flexeril, apo-cyclobenzaprin, fexmid, novo-cycloprine

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 5-10mg
- **Heavy:** 15mg+
- **Light:** 2-5mg
- **Strong:** 10-15mg

## Duration
- **Onset:** 15-45 minutes
- **Duration:** 6-12 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-6 hours
