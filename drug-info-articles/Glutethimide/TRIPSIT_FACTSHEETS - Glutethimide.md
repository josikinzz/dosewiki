# Glutethimide
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

The drug is a hypnotic sedative that was introduced in 1954, as a "safe" alternative to barbiturates to treat insomnia.

## Classification
- **Categories:** depressant, habit-forming
- **Also known as:** doriden, elrodorm, noxyron, glimid

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 500mg
- **Light:** 250mg
- **Strong:** 750mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** 30-45 minutes
- **Duration:** 2-8 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-12 hours

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** All other CNS depressants.
