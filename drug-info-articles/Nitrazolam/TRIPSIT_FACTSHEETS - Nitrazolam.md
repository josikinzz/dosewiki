# Nitrazolam
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Hypnotic benzodiazepine that is the triazolo analogue of Nitrazepam and about 4x more potent compared to the parent compound. It is also  dechlorinated derivative of clonazolam with roughly 1/10 potency.

## Classification
- **Categories:** benzodiazepine, habit-forming, depressant, tentative

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 1-2mg
- **Light:** 0.5-1mg
- **Strong:** 2-3mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** 15-30 minutes
- **Duration:** 5-10 hours
- **After Effects:** 2-24 hours

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** All other CNS depressants.
