# 6-MDDM
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Semisynthetic opioid that is a derivative of Hydromorphone with little history of human use. Roughly around ~80x the potency of Morphine. Structurally related to Desomorphine.

## Classification
- **Categories:** opioid, habit-forming, tentative, depressant
- **Also known as:** 6-methylenedihydrodesoxymorphine

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 187-250ug
- **Light:** 125ug
- **Strong:** 375ug+

*Note:  NOTE: These dosages are based off the potency of the drug and converted to what we have with morphine's dose. Further research is required for dosing.*

## Duration
- **Onset:** 1-15 minutes
- **Duration:** 4-6 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-10 hours

## Effects
- Euphoria
- Dry Mouth
- Mood lift
- Itchiness
- Relaxant
- Constipation
- Pupil constriction
- Analgesia

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** All other CNS depressants.
