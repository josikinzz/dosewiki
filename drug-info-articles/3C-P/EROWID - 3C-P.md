**U.S. FEDERAL LEGAL SUMMARY**
**3C-P**
- REGULATED: No
- STATUS: Not Approved ForHuman Consumption
- SCHEDULE: Un-Scheduled
 
3C-P is an uncontrolled substance in the U.S. as well as in most if not all other countries. It could conceivably be covered under the Analog Act depending on how it's sold, but there have been no cases prosecuted that we are aware of. (11/03/2004) If sold as a supplement, sales must conform to U.S. supplement laws. If sold for consumption as a food or drug, sales are regulated by the FDA.
