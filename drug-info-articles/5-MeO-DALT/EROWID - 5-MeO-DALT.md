DOSAGE DESCRIPTION #
 
Caution : Dose information should not be taken as recommendations. [ see below ]
 
**5-MeO-DALT Dosages**
**Oral**
- Threshold: 4 - 5 mg
- Light: 5 - 12 mg
- Common: 12 - 25 mg
- Strong: 25+ mg
 
5-MeO-DALT is a short-acting tryptamine. It is most often found in powder form that is taken orally or nasally.
 
NOTES #
 
The range of doses and effects reported for 5-MeO-DALT is unusually broad. Some reports for 5-MeO-DALT may actually be describing ingestion of other substances misrepresented as 5-MeO-DALT, complicating its profile.

U.S. FEDERAL LAW #

**U.S. FEDERAL LEGAL SUMMARY**
**5-MeO-DALT**
- REGULATED: No
- STATUS: Not Approved ForHuman Consumption
- SCHEDULE: Unscheduled
 
5-MeO-DALT is uncontrolled in the United States. This means it is generally considered legal to buy, possess, and distribute (sell, trade or give). If sold as a supplement, sales must conform to U.S. supplement laws. If sold for consumption as a food or drug, sales are regulated by the FDA.

INTERNATIONAL LAW #
 
China #
 
Controlled in China as a Category I psychotropic substance, illegal to sell, buy, import, export, and manufacture 4-methylmethcathinone as of September 2010. See [China's SFDA announcement](https://erowid.org/psychoactives/law/countries/china/china_law_2015_09_27_list_of_newly_controlled_chemicals.pdf) . (thanks n) (last updated Nov 25, 2015)
 
Finland #
 
5-MeO-DALT was banned in Finland in December 2014. A new government regulation was enacted in Finland that banned over 100 psychoactive chemicals. See [Finland's Prohibited Psychoactive Substances: December 19, 2014](https://erowid.org/psychoactives/law/countries/finland/finland_law1_2014.pdf) . (last updated Dec 26, 2014)
 
Japan #
 
5-MeO-DALT is controlled as a "Designated Substance" (Shitei-Yakubutsu) by the Pharmaceutical Affairs Law, making it illegal to possess or sell (see [Ref](https://erowid.org/references/refs_view.php?ID=7395) )
 
United Kingdom #
 
5-MeO-DALT was added to Class A in January 2015. See [https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/circular-0012015-a-change-to-the-misuse-of-drugs-act-1971-control-of-ah-7921-lsd-related-compounds-tryptamines-and-rescheduling-of-ghb](https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/circular-0012015-a-change-to-the-misuse-of-drugs-act-1971-control-of-ah-7921-lsd-related-compounds-tryptamines-and-rescheduling-of-ghb) . Previously it was in an ambiguous status, possibly falling under the generic tryptamine catch-all paragraph. (Thanks k) (last updated Mar 7 2015)
 
Sweden #
 
According to a Swedish online vendor, as of May 1, 2007, 5-MeO-DALT will be controlled in Sweden. Previously, it was not included in the list of controlled substances (Laekemedels Vaerket) (thanks Peter and VaPPa) (March 2007)
