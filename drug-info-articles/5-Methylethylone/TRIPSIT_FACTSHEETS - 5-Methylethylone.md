# 5-Methylethylone
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

The drug is also known as 5-methyl-ethylone, or 5-ME. This is a cathinone drug and a analogue of ethylone. The drug is suspected to be similar to other stimulants of the cathinone class, with a slightly higher potency than ethylone. The drug is possibly entactogenic and a monoamine releasing agent.

## Classification
- **Categories:** stimulant, research-chemical
- **Also known as:** 5-methyl-ethylone, 5-me

## Dosage

### Insufflated

- **Light:** 30-60mg
- **Strong:** 150-300mg+

### Oral

- **Light:** 50-100mg
