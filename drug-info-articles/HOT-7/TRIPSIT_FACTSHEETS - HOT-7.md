# HOT-7
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A rather rare psychedelic phenylethylamine that is the hydroxylated version and prodrug for 2C-T-7.

## Classification
- **Categories:** psychedelic, research-chemical, tentative, empathogen
- **Also known as:** hot7

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 20-30mg
- **Light:** 15-20mg
- **Strong:** 30-40mg+
- **Threshold:** 15mg

*Note:  NOTE: The dose curve is reported to be abnormal. As such, additional caution is advised when dosing. It is suggested that there may be MAOI effects as similiar with 2C-T-7.*

## Duration
- **Onset:** 60-180 minutes
- **Duration:** 5-8 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-12 hours

## Effects
- Euphoria
- empathy
- insight
- brightened colour
- Closed/Open eye visuals
- enhanced tactile sensation
- mental/physical stimulation
- decreased appetite
- pupil dilation
- restlessness
- change in perception
- ego softening
- sweating/chills
- muscle tension
- confusion
- insomnia
