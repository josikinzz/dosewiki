# Mephedrone
*Source: https://tripbot.tripsit.me/api/tripsit/getDrug/mephedrone*

## Classification
- **Categories:** stimulant, research-chemical, habit-forming, empathogen
- **Also known as:** 4-mmc, meow, m-cat, 4-methylmethcathinone, 4mmc

## Dosage

### Insufflated
- **Common:** 20-80mg
- **Heavy:** 125mg+
- **Light:** 15-25mg
- **Strong:** 75-125mg

### Oral
- **Common:** 100-200mg
- **Heavy:** 300mg+
- **Light:** 50-100mg
- **Strong:** 150-300mg

## Duration
- **Onset:** 15-45 minutes
- **Duration:** 2-3 hours
- **After Effects:** 6-24 hours

## Effects
- Generally described as being more intense of a stimulant than MDMA but with somewhat less of an empathogenic feel

## Interactions

### ⚠️ Dangerous
- **dextromethorphan**: Risk of serotonin syndrome as both drugs raise serotonin levels. In addition to concerns of high blood pressure and unnecessary strain on the heart. As well as potentially causing anxiety and greater physical discomfort due to the combination.
