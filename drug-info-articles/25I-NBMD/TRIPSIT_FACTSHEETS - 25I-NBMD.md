# 25I-NBMD
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A psychedelic phenethylamine that is related to the 25X-NBOMe series. The drug is highly potent.

## Classification
- **Categories:** psychedelic, research-chemical, tentative

## Dosage

### Sublingual
- **Common:** 1500-2000ug
- **Light:** 800-1500ug
- **Strong:** 2000-3000ug+

## Duration
- **Onset:** 20-40 minutes
- **Duration:** 4-6 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-8 hours

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** Other psychedelics, stimulants, and redosing
