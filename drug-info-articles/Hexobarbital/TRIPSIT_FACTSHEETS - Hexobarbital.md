# Hexobarbital
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

The drug is barbiturate that was used for anesthesia during surgery. The drug has a very rapid onset and a short duration of effects.

## Classification
- **Categories:** barbiturate, depressant, habit-forming, tentative

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 15-20mg
- **Light:** 10-15mg
- **Strong:** 20-30mg

*Note:  Note: These dosages are extremely tentative.*

## Duration
- **Onset:** 1-2 minutes
- **Duration:** 30-45 minutes
- **After Effects:** 1-4 hours

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** All CNS depressants.
