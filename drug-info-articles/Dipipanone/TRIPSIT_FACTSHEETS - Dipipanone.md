# Dipipanone
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A powerful opioid of equivalent strength to morphine, rarely seen outside of medical environments. Often used to treat severe pain where morphine is contraindicated. Usually found in combination with the antihistamine Cyclizine, which potentiates it.

## Classification
- **Categories:** opioid, depressant, habit-forming, depressant
- **Also known as:** diconal

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 5-10mg
- **Light:** 3-5mg
- **Strong:** 10-20mg+

## Duration
- **Onset:** minutes
- **Duration:** 3-6 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-12 hours

## Effects
- Euphoria
- Dry Mouth
- Mood lift
- Itchiness
- Relaxant
- Constipation
- Pupil constriction
- Analgesia

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** All other CNS depressants
