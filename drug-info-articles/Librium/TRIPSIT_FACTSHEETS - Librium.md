# Librium
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

Chlordiazepoxide, a medium-acting benzodiazepine drug typically prescribed for sleep purposes. The drug is reported to be sedative and hypnotic. The drug may  lower inhibitions and cause amnesia.

## Classification
- **Categories:** benzodiazepine, habit-forming, depressant
- **Also known as:** chlordiazepoxide

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 10-20mg
- **Heavy:** 20-40mg+
- **Light:** 5-10mg

*Note:  NOTE: 25mg of Librium is approximately equal to 10mg of Diazepam*

## Duration
- **Onset:** 20-40 minutes
- **Duration:** 6-10 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-14 hours

## Effects
- Anxiolytic
- Sedative
- Muscle Relaxant
- Amnesia
- Dystaxia
- Hypnotic

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** All other CNS depressants
