# DOIP
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A rare psychedelic ampthetamine of the same class as the likes of DOM. Is a quite powerful and potent psychedelic that has very strong amphetamine effects. Not for the faint of heart.

## Classification
- **Categories:** tentative, psychedelic, stimulant, research-chemical

## Dosage

### Oral
- **Common:** 1.5-3mg
- **Light:** 800-1500ug
- **Strong:** 3mg+
- **Threshold:** 800ug

*Note:  Note: This is based on personal experience as no trip reports have surfaced in the three years it's been on the market, so take with caution.*

## Duration
- **Onset:** 45-120 minutes
- **Duration:** 18-30 hours
- **After Effects:** 1-48 hours

## Additional Information
- **Avoid:** Double dosing as it has a fairly long onset. Also any stimulant that's stronger than caffeine, and even then it can get quite uncomfortable.
