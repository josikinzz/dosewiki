# Mebroqualone
*Source: TripSit Factsheets (tripsit.me)*

## Summary

A short-acting more potent analogue of Methaqualone.

## Classification
- **Categories:** depressant, research-chemical, habit-forming, tentative
- **Also known as:** mbq

## Dosage

### Sublingual
- **Common:** 8-15mg
- **Light:** 5-8mg
- **Strong:** 15-20mg.
- **Threshold:** 3-5mg

### Vapourized
- **Common:** 5-10mg
- **Light:** 3-5mg
- **Strong:** 10-20mg+
- **Threshold:** 2-3mg

## Duration
- **Onset:** minutes
- **Duration:** minutes
- **After Effects:** 1-4 hours

## Effects
- Anxiolytic
- Sedative
- Muscle Relaxant
- Amnesia
- Dystaxia
- Hypnotic
